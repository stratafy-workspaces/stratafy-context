# Aligned AI Deal Workspace validates Stratafy's core architecture patterns

## Summary
Aligned (launched Feb 11 2026) proved that bounded-context AI workspaces with proactive signal detection measurably shorten cycles and increase win rates in B2B sales. Their architecture — single persistent workspace, bounded AI context, engagement signal analytics, embedded playbooks — maps directly to what Stratafy is building for strategy execution. This is market validation from an adjacent vertical.

## Description
Aligned's AI Deal Workspace solves the sales equivalent of Stratafy's strategy-execution gap: the space between the CRM (system of record) and where real decisions happen (the buyer's messy, invisible process). Their key architectural choices validate Stratafy's approach: (1) Single shared workspace beats fragmented tools, (2) Bounded context makes AI trustworthy for enterprise, (3) Proactive signal detection closes visibility gaps, (4) Participant-led systems outperform owner-orchestrated ones. The differentiation opportunity: Stratafy is the enterprise-wide, multi-agent version of what Aligned built for sales — with full organizational graph, MCP-bounded context, and cross-functional coordination. The OpenClaw security narrative is a ready-made counter-positioning opportunity: "We give agents the same bounded, governed context — but across your entire strategy."
