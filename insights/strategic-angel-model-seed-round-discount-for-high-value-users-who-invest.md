# Strategic angel model — seed round discount for high-value users who invest

## Summary
Offer select strategic people a seed round discount in exchange for active Stratafy usage. Creates investor-users who are financially and operationally aligned — curated cap table over volume, turning fundraising into simultaneous GTM and validation.

## Description
Concept: offer a select group of strategic people a discount on the seed round in exchange for actively using Stratafy in their businesses. This creates a class of investor-users who are both financially and operationally aligned. They generate real usage data, provide authentic product feedback, serve as case studies, and have financial incentive to advocate. The cap table stays intentionally small and curated — not seeking volume of investors but quality of strategic alignment. Candidates include EO Forum members like Rich and Brad who already understand the product and have businesses that would benefit. This model turns fundraising into a GTM and validation exercise simultaneously.
