## Summary
Henniel's excitement about the Alignment Suite validates it as a sellable capability, not just an internal feature. His scorecards are snapshots; Stratafy makes alignment continuous.

## Description
Henniel Smit (Limbik Strategy Advisors) demo on Feb 24 revealed the Alignment Suite as the highest-resonance feature for strategy consultants who focus on executive alignment. Henniel's methodology is built around alignment scorecards — point-in-time assessments of executive team alignment before moving to execution. Stratafy's alignment scanning makes this continuous rather than episodic, which is a direct upgrade to his existing practice.

This could be a demo hook for every strategy advisory firm that does alignment work.

Henniel also represents the "marketing strategy → corporate strategy" expansion pattern. He's leveling up his practice and needs infrastructure for the more complex engagements. Stratafy enables that transition. This is a repeatable segment: established consultants expanding their scope who need more sophisticated tooling.
