# Alignment scan value is in cross-strategy synthesis, not individual reviews

## Summary
Individual alignment scans score 7-9 and are useful. But the real insight emerges when you synthesize across all 7 reviews: "Craft is unprotected across 5 of 7 strategies" — a pattern no individual review surfaces. The synthesis layer is the product differentiator.

## Description
During the STIR Collective engagement, we ran 7 individual alignment scans (one per strategy). Each produced valuable findings. But the transformative moment was manually synthesizing all 7 into a consolidated report that revealed cross-cutting patterns: Craft unprotected (5/7), Skills Development disconnected (4/7), Build Don't Rent inconsistently applied (3/7). No human reviewer would naturally check "does your fundraising strategy protect your craft values?" — but cross-strategy synthesis asks exactly these questions. This validates that the report synthesis feature (STR-94 through STR-101) is a critical product capability, not a nice-to-have. The synthesis is where the "strategic intelligence" claim becomes real.
