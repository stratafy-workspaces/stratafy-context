# The timing inversion — structure before execution, not after — is the universal pain point across all alignment domains

## Summary
Every alignment domain shares the same root problem: operational structure is set up after strategy is already in motion. Stratafy inverts this by proposing structure at the moment of strategic creation. This timing inversion is the core value proposition across finance, org design, AI governance, and all other domains.

## Description
The timing problem in finance — that GL accounts are set up after strategy is in motion rather than before — is the sharpest articulation of why retroactive alignment fails and proactive alignment is necessary. This framing applies universally: teams are assembled after initiatives launch, metrics are defined after objectives are set, meetings are scheduled after priorities shift, AI agent permissions are configured after deployment. In every domain, the structural response lags the strategic decision. Stratafy's value proposition is inverting this sequence across all eight operational domains, ensuring structure is proposed at the moment of strategic creation rather than retrofitted afterward.
