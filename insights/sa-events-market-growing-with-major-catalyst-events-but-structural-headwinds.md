# SA events market growing with major catalyst events but structural headwinds

## Summary
Virtual events: $636M to $2B+ by 2030 (21.8% CAGR). G20, Cricket World Cup 2027, F1 ambitions as catalysts. Headwinds: load shedding, skills gaps, visa delays.

## Description
SA virtual events market generated $636.6M in 2024, projected to reach $2.08B by 2030 at 21.8% CAGR. The broader SA events market grows at 5.1% CAGR. Key catalysts include G20 Summit (Nov 2025), Cricket World Cup co-hosting (2027), and F1 race ambitions. SA ranks #1 conference destination in Africa/Middle East. However, challenges include government spending constraints, load shedding, skills shortages, slow visa processing, and rising East African competition from Kigali, Nairobi, and Addis Ababa.
