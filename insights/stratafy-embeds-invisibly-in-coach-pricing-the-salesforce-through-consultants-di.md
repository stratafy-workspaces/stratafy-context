# Stratafy embeds invisibly in coach pricing — the "Salesforce-through-consultants" distribution model works

## Summary
The proposal naturally embedded Stratafy as coaching infrastructure inside Nicholas's retainer, proving the indirect distribution and retention thesis.

## Description
The proposal naturally positioned Stratafy as the ongoing coaching infrastructure — "Async support via Stratafy workspace" appeared as a line item in Nicholas's engagement. This reveals a critical pricing/positioning insight: Stratafy doesn't need to sell directly to STIR. Nicholas includes Stratafy as part of his coaching toolkit, and STIR pays Nicholas. The coach becomes the distribution channel AND the retention mechanism. The R45K/month coaching retainer includes Stratafy usage — meaning Stratafy's value is embedded in the coach's pricing, not sold separately. This is the "Salesforce-through-consultants" model. Coaches won't churn off Stratafy because it's woven into their client delivery. Clients won't churn because the coach is accountable for keeping the workspace alive. The triangle (Stratafy ↔ Coach ↔ Client) creates mutual lock-in that no single relationship would.
