# Potential case study opportunity: Rob's developer contact needs business strategy for tech product (April)

## Summary
Rob has a developer contact who built a tech product but struggles with revenue model and business strategy. They agreed to use Stratafy to help define the strategy starting April. This is a potential proof point — different persona (tech founder) than STIR (creative company).

## Description
Rob Horton asked if Stratafy could be used to help a developer he's mentoring who has built a tech product but is struggling with the revenue model and business strategy. Leonard agreed to collaborate starting April.

Strategic value of this opportunity:
1. **Second use case** — different from STIR Collective (creative/media company). A tech founder struggling with business strategy is a common persona.
2. **Rob as channel partner test** — Rob is essentially introducing Stratafy to someone in his network. This tests the referral/advisor channel.
3. **AI-assisted strategy generation showcase** — this is exactly the "workspace from a URL" use case. Start with the product, generate the strategy. Could become a powerful demo.
4. **Proof point for seed raise** — "we helped a tech founder go from product-only to defined strategy in X hours using AI" is a compelling story.

Risk: this is unpaid and informal. Set clear expectations about scope and what success looks like.

Action: Schedule kickoff with Rob and the developer for early April. Treat it as a structured case study with before/after measurements. Document the process for repeatable playbook.
