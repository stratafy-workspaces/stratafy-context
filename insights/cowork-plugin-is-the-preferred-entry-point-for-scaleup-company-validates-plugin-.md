# Cowork plugin is the preferred entry point for ScaleUp Company — validates plugin as GTM accelerator

## Summary
Nicholas Thiede's excitement about Claude Cowork suggests the plugin isn't just a product feature — it's the interface that makes adoption frictionless for coaches who already live in Claude.

## Description
ScaleUp Company demo (Feb 23) revealed that Nicholas is most excited about the Cowork plugin angle. He wants to use Stratafy through Cowork, not through the web UI. This is a significant signal: the Cowork plugin may be the primary adoption interface for coaches, not the dashboard. The implication is that the Cowork plugin initiative isn't just a product feature — it's a GTM accelerator. Coaches who already use Claude for their work will discover Stratafy through the plugin, build their own strategy first, then bring clients. This is the same "dogfood then distribute" pattern emerging with Too Many Robots. Priority implication: getting the Cowork plugin stable and polished should be treated as a sales enablement priority, not just a product roadmap item.
