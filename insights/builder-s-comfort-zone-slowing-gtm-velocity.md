# Builder's comfort zone slowing GTM velocity

## Summary
Coaching revealed a pattern of choosing product building over delivering to prospects. Brad Wilson is ready for his workspace + radar report — the plugin isn't a prerequisite. The instinct to "show scope and vision" and polish before delivering is creating artificial delays in pipeline conversion.

## Description
During an end-of-day coaching session, Leonard identified that his GTM velocity blocker isn't product readiness — it's a comfort preference for building over the uncertainty of putting the product in someone's hands. Specific case: Brad Wilson was promised a workspace setup and radar report last week, but delivery was delayed while the Cowork plugin was being built. The plugin isn't required to deliver on the promise. Commitment made: set up Brad's workspace tomorrow morning, schedule radar report before his strategy session next week.
