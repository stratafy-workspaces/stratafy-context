# Stratafy's strategic intelligence layer is the 'intelligence component' that Sequoia says will be automated out of management consulting

## Summary
Sequoia's copilot-to-autopilot framework validates Stratafy's positioning: we automate the intelligence layer of strategic work (context delivery, signal processing, alignment tracking) while preserving the judgement layer for human leaders. The 'disaggregate consulting' thesis is essentially a description of our product strategy.

## Description
Sequoia's "Services: The New Software" article argues that AI will disaggregate management consulting into intelligence components (automatable) and judgement components (human). Stratafy is already building exactly this — infrastructure that powers the intelligence layer of strategic work while the judgement layer stays with human leaders. This validates our Advisory Firms as Customer Segment and Fractional Executive channel strategies as the outsourced wedge. The copilot-to-autopilot convergence framework also maps onto our product trajectory from strategic tools to strategic outcomes.
