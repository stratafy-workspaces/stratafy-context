# Eight alignment domains identified for Continuous Strategic Execution Alignment architecture

## Summary
Eight operational domains follow the same alignment loop: Finance, Org Design, AI Governance, Product/Engineering, Risk & Compliance, Calendar & Time, Decisions & Knowledge, and Metrics & Measurement. Each adds independent value; together they create the compound effect that justifies the strategic operating system positioning.

## Description
Stratafy has identified eight operational domains where the derive → monitor → correct alignment loop applies: (1) Finance — COA proposals, mappings, IFRS/GAAP compliance; (2) Organizational Design — team structures, role proposals, capability gaps; (3) AI/Agent Governance — permissions, context boundaries, tool access; (4) Product/Engineering — roadmap alignment, investment justification; (5) Risk & Compliance — risk register alignment, regulatory coverage; (6) Calendar & Time — meeting cadence, time allocation, capacity conflicts; (7) Decisions & Knowledge — decision context validity, knowledge gap detection; (8) Metrics & Measurement — KPI alignment, measurement gaps, metric orphan detection. Each domain independently adds value but the compound effect of all eight scanning against a single strategy tree is what creates the platform moat.
