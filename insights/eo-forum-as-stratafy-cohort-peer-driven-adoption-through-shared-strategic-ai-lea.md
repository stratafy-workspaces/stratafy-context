# EO Forum as Stratafy cohort — peer-driven adoption through shared strategic AI learning

## Summary
Turn the EO Rubber Ducks Forum into a Stratafy cohort where each member runs it for their business. Rich and Brad as early movers, with the forum itself becoming a peer-driven adoption engine and potential replicable model for other forums.

## Description
Vision: get each EO Rubber Ducks Forum member running Stratafy for their own business strategy. This turns the forum into a live proving ground for AI-native strategy — members share learnings, compare approaches, and collectively build confidence in using AI for strategic decision-making. The forum's existing trust, accountability structure, and regular cadence make it an ideal adoption environment. Starting with Richard Mulholland (Missing Link) and Brad as early movers creates social proof within the group. This could become a powerful forum tool in its own right — and a replicable model for other EO forums and peer advisory groups. The broader implication is that EO forums, YPO chapters, and similar peer groups could be a cohort-based GTM motion for Stratafy, distinct from but complementary to the coach channel.
