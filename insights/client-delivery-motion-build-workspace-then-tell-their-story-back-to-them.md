# Client delivery motion — build workspace, then tell their story back to them

## Summary
Build someone's workspace, then immediately walk them through their own strategy in story mode — their mission, their bets, their initiatives rendered and narrated. That moment of seeing their own strategy told compellingly is the activation event. The question they're left with becomes 'how do I make this even better?' not 'how do I use this?'

## Description
The current client delivery motion — build workspace, hand over access, explain the interface — creates a low-urgency handoff. The client has a workspace but hasn't felt why it matters yet.

An alternative delivery motion changes this completely:
1. Build the workspace as normal
2. Before handing over, prepare the story view — select the most relevant audience mode (investor, team, board) and walk the client through their own strategy, narrated
3. The client sees their mission, their bets, their initiatives, their risks — rendered beautifully and told as a coherent story
4. They experience what it would feel like to present this to their board or investors
5. The urgency to keep it current becomes self-evident

This motion turns the workspace handover into a moment of realisation rather than a training session. The question the client is left with is not 'how do I use this?' but 'how do I make this even better?'

For STIR specifically: walking Tracey through their strategy in the investor or team mode — showing their R100K MRR target, their team activation metrics, their initiatives, their risks — in a visual narrative view would be a more powerful retention moment than any feature walkthrough.

This also has a direct implication for the case study: the deliverable is not just 'we built them a workspace.' The deliverable is 'we built them a workspace and they can now tell their strategy story to any audience in minutes.' That's a qualitatively different product claim.

Broader implication for GTM: advisory firms and fractional executives who use this motion with their own clients get to show up to every engagement with a beautiful, narrated strategy story built from the client's own data. That's the 'leave an executive team behind' value prop made tangible and visceral.
