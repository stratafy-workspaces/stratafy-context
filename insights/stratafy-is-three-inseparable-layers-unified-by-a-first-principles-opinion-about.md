# Stratafy is three inseparable layers unified by a first-principles opinion about strategy

## Summary
Stratafy is not a platform, not infrastructure, not a SaaS tool. Three inseparable layers: (1) opinionated domain schema — typed strategic entities with domain semantics; (2) platform of tools built on top; (3) continuously operating system — cron jobs, health checks, distillation, context packaging by role. The layers are only valuable together. No generic infrastructure player can replicate this from the outside in — the opinion must be structural from the start.

## Description
Stratafy is most precisely defined as the combination of three inseparable layers, unified by a single first-principles opinion about what strategy is.

**Layer 1 — Infrastructure (first principles)**
The opinionated domain schema. Typed entities with domain semantics that no generic memory or AI infrastructure system has: risks with likelihood × impact scores, assumptions with confidence levels, decisions classified as Type 1/Type 2, metrics with polarity and thresholds, signals with sentiment and magnitude. The Foundation → Strategy → Initiatives → Objectives hierarchy. The intelligence layers and their typed relationships. This is not configurable — it is the structural encoding of how strategy actually works. It is the foundation everything else sits on, and it cannot be replicated from the infrastructure side up.

**Layer 2 — Platform**
The tools, interfaces, and workflows built on top of the infrastructure. Chat, Alignment Scanning, Radar, Cortex, Story Mode, InsightSync, Pulse, Context Matrix, Graph, Finance, Organogram, MCP, Bot. These surfaces are how humans and AI agents interact with and act on the infrastructure. They are opinionated expressions of the infrastructure — not independent features.

**Layer 3 — Operating System**
The continuous processes that keep everything alive without being asked. Distillation cron (change-aware, every 5 minutes), health scoring, content quality flags, role-based context packaging, permission governance, semantic embedding, staleness detection, alignment scanning. This layer is what separates Stratafy from a well-designed database with a UI. The system runs, detects, updates, and surfaces continuously — it is alive.

**Why the combination is the definition**
The three layers are only valuable together. Infrastructure without the operating layer goes stale. A platform without the infrastructure competes on features and loses. An operating layer without domain infrastructure has nothing meaningful to operate on. Generic AI infrastructure plays (HydraDB, Mem0, Zep) have Layer 3 primitives. None have Layer 1 opinion. They cannot arrive at what Stratafy is by adding domain logic later — the opinion must be structural from the start.

**The definition:**
Stratafy is an opinionated strategic intelligence system — first-principles infrastructure for how strategy works, a platform of tools built on top, and a continuously operating layer that keeps strategic context alive, governed, and consumable by both humans and AI agents.
