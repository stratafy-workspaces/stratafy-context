# Methodology networks are Stratafy's GTM channel — coaches upgrade from facilitators to intelligence interpreters across all frameworks

## Summary
Traditional methodologies are event-based in a continuous world. Stratafy's real competitors are documents and coach memory. Advisory firms across all methodology networks (EOS, Scaling Up, OKR, BSC, 4DX) are the GTM channel — coaches upgrade from facilitators to intelligence interpreters. TAM: millions of methodology users.

## Description
SYNTHESIZED FROM 4 INSIGHTS (Feb 9-10, 2026): 553b3dbe, 8d7bf583, 675972f5, 69360468

CORE THESIS: Traditional strategy methodologies (Scaling Up, EOS, OKRs, BSC, 4DX, Blue Ocean) are structurally obsolete — they treat strategy as events (offsites, quarterly reviews) in a world that requires continuous, contextual, compounding strategy. Every methodology produces artifacts that drift between sessions. The OPSP goes in a drawer, the V/TO updates annually, OKR spreadsheets lose context.

REAL COMPETITORS: Stratafy's competitors aren't other tools — they're documents, spreadsheets, and coach memories. Positioning must be additive: "we don't replace your methodology, we make it persist." This removes the biggest adoption objection before it's raised.

COACH VALUE TRANSFORMATION: Stratafy doesn't kill methodology coaches — it upgrades their role from facilitating quarterly sessions to interpreting continuous intelligence. Their value moves from running the framework to interpreting what the system generates. Better business for them: recurring infrastructure revenue instead of periodic project fees.

CHANNEL EXPANSION: The advisory channel extends beyond Scaling Up to every methodology network: EOS Implementers, 4DX consultants (FranklinCovey), OKR coaches, BSC practitioners (Palladium). Every delivery network has the same between-session visibility problem. One advisor = many client deployments.

TAM: 80,000+ Scaling Up users, 250,000+ EOS users, millions on OKRs. The channel strategy isn't "Scaling Up coaches" — it's "every advisory firm delivering any strategy methodology."

PITCH TO SCALEUP: "The world changed. Methodology sessions can't keep up. Here's what replaces the gap between sessions — and it makes your business more valuable, not less."
