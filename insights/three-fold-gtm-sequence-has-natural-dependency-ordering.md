# Three-fold GTM sequence has natural dependency ordering

## Summary
GTM stages (advisory → coach → self-serve) have natural dependencies; each de-risks the next with required artifacts.

## Description
The GTM sequence of (1) founder-led advisory, (2) coach/consultant channel, (3) self-serve platform follows a natural dependency chain. Advisory validates the value proposition and builds case studies. Coach channel validates that the platform can deliver value without founder involvement. Self-serve validates that the product can stand alone. Each stage de-risks the next and produces the artifacts (case studies, playbooks, onboarding flows) needed for the subsequent stage. Trying to skip stages or run them in parallel increases risk significantly.
