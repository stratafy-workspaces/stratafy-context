## Summary
For experiential products, showing beats telling. Make live working sessions the standard sales motion. If they feel it, they're sold.

## Description
For an experiential product like Stratafy, a slide deck can't convey the value. The case study is proof; the live experience is the close. Build the muscle of doing live demos with prospect's actual context.
