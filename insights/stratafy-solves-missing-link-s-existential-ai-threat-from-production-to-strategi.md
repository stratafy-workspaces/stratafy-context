# Stratafy solves Missing Link's existential AI threat — from production to strategic storytelling

## Summary
AI presentation tools are commoditising Missing Link's production layer. Stratafy enables their shift to strategic storytelling by giving them deep client strategy context. Stratafy becomes both their internal tool and their competitive moat against AI disruption.

## Description
Missing Link faces a fundamental threat: AI presentation tools are commoditising slide production, the layer that's been their core business for 25 years. Their survival requires moving up the value chain from production to strategic storytelling. Stratafy directly enables this shift — if clients have their strategy structured in Stratafy, Missing Link can deliver presentations grounded in deep strategic context rather than shallow briefing calls. This positions Missing Link as the firm that starts with strategy and uses AI to execute production, while competitors race to the bottom on AI-generated slides. Stratafy becomes both Missing Link's internal strategic tool AND the mechanism that makes their client work defensible. This is the strongest possible framing for the Monday meeting with Rich — it's not just "use our tool," it's "this is how Missing Link survives and thrives in the AI era."
