# Content pillar audit reveals distribution imbalance and demand-gen gap

## Summary
29 articles across 7 pillars, but 12 of 29 (41%) sit in just 2 pillars. Three weakest pillars (Stage-Specific, Compounding, MCP/Technical) have only 6 posts combined despite being the most differentiated. Critical gap: zero case studies, zero implementation guides — content is conceptual, not demand-generating.

## Description
Full pillar audit conducted Feb 27. Key findings: (1) Strong pillars (Execution Gap: 5, AI Governance: 7) reflect instinctive category narrative but are reaching diminishing returns. (2) Underdeveloped pillars (Stage-Specific: 2, Compounding: 2, MCP/Technical: 2) contain the most differentiated thinking and are closest to customer pain points from demos. (3) Biggest cross-pillar gap is demand-generation content: case studies with real data, implementation guides, competitive comparisons. (4) 169 MCP tools built but only 2 technical articles — massive underleveraged asset. (5) Phase 0 (conceptual thought leadership) is complete. Phase 1 must shift to "here's what we did" content using STIR/ScaleUp proof points.
