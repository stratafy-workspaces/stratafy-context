# Financial Alignment Scan as 6th lens in Strategic Alignment Suite

## Summary
Financial Alignment Scan — 6th lens checking if COA/financial structure reflects strategic commitments. Completes the suite: Intent, Coherence, Reality, Perception, Capacity, Resources.

## Description
Emerged from building Stratafy's own Chart of Accounts: the Financial Alignment Scan evaluates whether a company's financial structure (COA, cost classification, revenue segmentation) reflects its strategic commitments. This is the 6th lens in the Strategic Alignment Suite, completing the model: Intent + Coherence + Reality + Perception + Capacity + Resources. No strategy tool in the market offers this — it bridges CFO and CSO concerns in a single scan. The pitch becomes: "Six lenses. One engine." Key perspective types: revenue_alignment, cost_alignment, ai_economics, channel_economics, fx_exposure, fundraise_readiness, entity_structure. Particularly powerful for AI-native companies where compute cost classification (COGS vs. OpEx) directly shapes the investor narrative.
