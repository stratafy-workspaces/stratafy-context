# STIR employee count discrepancy and geographic concentration need validation

## Summary
VP Africa shows 201-500 employees vs group ZoomInfo of 11-50. All operations centralised in Midrand vs Gearhouse's 3-city presence. Both need validation.

## Description
Virtual Productions Africa lists 201-500 employees on LinkedIn while ZoomInfo shows STIR Collective group at 11-50 employees. This discrepancy needs clarification during the engagement. If VP Africa truly has 200+ employees, the collective is much larger than surface-level data suggests and the prospectus should reflect this scale. Additionally, STIR operates entirely from Midrand while Gearhouse has offices in Johannesburg, Cape Town, and Durban. Geographic expansion to Cape Town (Africa's top convention city) could be a strategic recommendation.
