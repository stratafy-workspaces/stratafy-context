# Coach deployments are the channel-scalability test

## Summary
Coach channel proves platform scalability beyond founder-led sales; key metric is independent client onboarding.

## Description
If we can successfully deploy Stratafy through business coaches and strategy consultants, it proves the platform can scale beyond founder-led sales. Coaches are the ideal channel because they already have the trust relationship, they need tools to systematize their advice, and they benefit from the recurring-revenue model. The key metric is whether a coach can onboard a client without Stratafy team involvement — that's the channel-scalability proof point.
