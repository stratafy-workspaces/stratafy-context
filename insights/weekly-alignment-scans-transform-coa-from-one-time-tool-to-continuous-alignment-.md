# Weekly alignment scans transform COA from one-time tool to continuous alignment engine

## Summary
Running COA alignment on a weekly cadence with automated alerts for new strategies, orphaned accounts, and mapping gaps creates operational stickiness and expands the user surface to finance teams who have weekly reasons to engage with Stratafy.

## Description
Moving the COA alignment from a one-time generation tool to a continuous weekly scan with alerts transforms the value proposition. When a strategy is activated, the next scan proposes GL accounts before the first dollar is spent — eliminating retroactive reclassification entirely. When strategies are archived, orphaned accounts are flagged for consolidation. This continuous cadence is what makes Stratafy operational infrastructure rather than a planning tool. It puts Stratafy into the weekly rhythm of the finance team, expanding the user surface from strategy leaders to FP&A, controllers, and CFOs. More users, more frequent engagement, harder to rip out.
