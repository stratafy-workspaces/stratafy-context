# Corporate AI projects underdelivering — validates Stratafy thesis

## Summary
Radar scan shows corporate AI investments not paying off yet. Dual signal: validates Stratafy's strategic infrastructure thesis while flagging potential AI fatigue risk in the market.

## Description
Radar scan revealed that corporate AI projects are broadly underdelivering on expected ROI. This validates Stratafy's thesis that the execution gap — not the AI capability gap — is the real problem. Organizations lack strategic infrastructure to connect AI initiatives to measurable outcomes. This is both a market risk (AI fatigue could slow adoption) and a positioning opportunity (Stratafy as the layer that makes AI investments pay off).
