# Stratafy as the single environment — think, work, present, all without leaving

## Summary
The full product vision: Stratafy is the single environment for the entire strategy lifecycle. You think in it, work in it, maintain it, and present from it — without ever leaving. Every transition between tools today is friction and potential drift between what you said and what's actually in the system.

## Description
Currently the strategy lifecycle is fragmented across tools:
- **Thinking** happens in Claude or in a doc
- **Storing** happens in Stratafy
- **Presenting** happens in slides or a manually assembled document
- **Reviewing** happens in a meeting with screenshots or exports

Every transition between these environments is friction. More critically, every transition is a potential point of drift — what you said in the pitch doesn't match what's in the workspace, what's in the workspace doesn't reflect the conversation you just had.

The product vision that resolves this: **Stratafy is the single environment where all of this happens**.

- The AI assistant is embedded, so thinking happens in context — every conversation is already aware of your strategy, your metrics, your risks, your assumptions
- The strategy tree is the canvas, so work lands directly in the system — no copy-paste from Claude into Stratafy
- Story Mode is the output layer, so presenting requires zero preparation — select audience, walk through, done
- Reviews happen inside the workspace, so insights and decisions are captured where they belong

This is a fundamentally different product category. Strategy tools are databases (Notion, Confluence), or presentation tools (slides), or execution trackers (Linear). None of them are the **thinking and working environment**. Stratafy can be all three if the friction between modes is eliminated.

**The activation implication:** if you can demo this in a single session — "I'm going to think through something with you, watch it land in the workspace, and then walk you through it in Story Mode" — the product sells itself. The gap between the current state (scattered, fragmented, high-effort) and the experience becomes visceral.

**What this requires:**
1. Embedded AI assistant that writes directly to the workspace — not a separate tab
2. Story Mode as a first-class output from within the same view
3. Frictionless mode switching: working mode ↔ story mode without leaving the page
4. The session is the record — conversations captured as decisions, insights, and strategy updates automatically
