# Customer insights in strategy sessions — potential customer persona module

## Summary
EO Forum feedback highlighted that having customer insights available during strategy sessions would be highly valuable. Most strategy sessions operate on assumptions about customers rather than actual intelligence. This suggests a product opportunity: a customer persona module that feeds real customer data and insights into the strategic layer, making strategy sessions grounded in reality rather than guesswork.

## Description
During the EO Forum Deep Dive (March 2026), attendees noted that having customer insights available during strategy sessions would significantly improve the quality of strategic decisions. Currently, most teams go into strategy sessions with assumptions about their customers — who they are, what they want, what they struggle with — rather than structured, up-to-date intelligence.

Product opportunity — Customer Persona Module:
A module that captures, structures, and maintains customer personas as a living part of the strategy. This could include:
- Customer segments with attributes, needs, and pain points
- Real customer feedback and quotes mapped to personas
- Buying triggers and objections per persona
- How each persona maps to strategies and initiatives
- Signals and insights filtered by customer relevance

This fits naturally into Stratafy's architecture:
- Lives alongside or within the Foundation layer (who are we serving?)
- Feeds into Strategies (how do we serve them?)
- Connects to Insights & Signals (what are we learning about them?)
- The AI experts could use persona context to make more relevant recommendations — e.g., the CMO expert could reference actual customer language when reviewing messaging alignment

The value proposition is clear: "Your strategy sessions are only as good as your understanding of your customer. Don't guess — know."

This also connects to the PMF search: if Stratafy can help founders define not just their strategy but their customer understanding, it becomes the foundation for everything — product decisions, marketing, sales, hiring.

Next steps: Explore what a minimum viable customer persona module looks like within the existing Stratafy architecture. Could start as a document type or a new strategic layer.
