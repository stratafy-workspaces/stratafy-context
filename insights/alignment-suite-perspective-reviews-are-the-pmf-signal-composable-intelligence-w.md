# Alignment Suite & Perspective Reviews are the PMF signal — composable intelligence with cross-strategy synthesis as the defensible moat

## Summary
Synthesized from 15 L1 insights. The Alignment Suite (6 lenses, 49 perspectives) and Perspective Reviews are Stratafy's "aha moment" features. The moat is cross-strategy synthesis catching misalignment humans structurally cannot detect. N capabilities produce N² value through composable intelligence. Three product gaps to close: batch scanning, deeper context consumption, and report-to-action loops.

## Description
Sources: 15 insights across stratafy_chat sessions and STIR engagement.

Key validated properties:
1. Cross-strategy synthesis is the moat — individual scans score 7-9 but systemic patterns emerge only from cross-strategy views (e.g., "Craft unprotected in 5/7 strategies")
2. Perspective Reviews are the "aha moment" — "Run your strategy through 8 functional lenses" is understandable in one sentence
3. Composable intelligence — each capability enriches all others through shared context (N² value)
4. Can't-fail property — bad reviews still produce value through dismissal rationale
5. Advisory firm productization — coaches ARE human perspective reviewers; Stratafy automates between sessions
6. Alignment Score as retention KPI — scan → action → re-scan → show improvement creates the return loop

Critical product gaps:
- Batch scanning: "Scan All & Generate Report" as single action (not 7 manual steps)
- Context depth: scans should consume initiatives, objectives, and metrics — not just strategy descriptions
- Action loop: reports must enable one-click initiative creation from recommendations
- Consultant alignment: reports become branded advisory deliverables
