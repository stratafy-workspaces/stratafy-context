# Stratafy gives coaches much earlier insight into their clients

## Summary
EO Forum feedback surfaced that Stratafy could give strategy coaches and consultants much earlier access to client insights — before the first session rather than during it. This opens a potential user segment (coaches/consultants) and a distribution channel: if the coach uses Stratafy with clients, the client adopts Stratafy as the infrastructure for the engagement.

## Description
During the EO Forum Deep Dive (March 2026), it was noted that Stratafy would give coaches much earlier insights into their customers (i.e., the coach's clients).

Currently, coaches and strategy consultants typically discover what a client knows about their own business during the first session. There's a ramp-up period where the coach is learning the client's context — their market, customers, team, challenges — before they can add real value. This is expensive time for both parties.

With Stratafy, the client's strategy is already structured and accessible before the first session. The coach could review the client's foundation, strategies, initiatives, metrics, insights, and signals ahead of time. They arrive already understanding the business — and can focus the session on what matters rather than on context-gathering.

This has several implications:

1. New user segment — Coaches, consultants, EOS implementers, Scaling Up facilitators. They're not the end customer but they could drive adoption.

2. Distribution channel — If the coach requires or recommends Stratafy as part of their engagement, every new client becomes a Stratafy user. The coach is the distribution mechanism.

3. Product angle — A "coach view" or "advisor view" that gives an external advisor read access to a client's strategic context. This connects to the existing Strategic Advisor expert role.

4. Retention angle — If the strategy lives in Stratafy, the client keeps using it between coaching sessions. The coach engagement ends but the infrastructure stays. This is the opposite of what happens now (coach leaves, strategy doc dies).

5. Connects to the customer persona insight — if customer intelligence is structured in Stratafy, the coach sees it too. They can challenge assumptions about customers with actual data from day one.

This could be a powerful go-to-market wedge: coaches as early adopters who bring their clients with them.
