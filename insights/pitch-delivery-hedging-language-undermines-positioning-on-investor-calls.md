# Pitch delivery: hedging language undermines positioning on investor calls

## Summary
Evolve call revealed excessive hedging in pitch delivery. Fix: lead with conviction, frame traction positively, anchor raise number specifically, ask sharper qualifying questions.

## Description
Self-assessment from Evolve intro call: excessive hedging undermined positioning. Phrases like "might be too early for you guys," "it's not much" (re: first customer revenue), "maybe a bit less" (re: raise size), and wide $2-5M range without rationale all signaled uncertainty rather than conviction. The strongest moment was the reframe toward portfolio visibility — shifting from being evaluated to creating value. Key improvement: lead with conviction, qualify with honesty second. Frame traction positively ("signed first customer within weeks of going to market" vs "it's not much"). Anchor the raise with a specific number and rationale. Ask sharper qualifying questions of intermediaries.
