# Autonomous strategy evolution — Stratafy should continuously improve strategy from signals

## Summary
Forum members expect that a system holding your strategy and tracking signals should autonomously evolve and improve the strategy — closing the loop from signal to insight to strategic adjustment without waiting for human initiation.

## Description
EO Forum feedback surfaced the expectation that if Stratafy holds your full strategy and continuously ingests signals, it should be able to autonomously refine and evolve the strategy over time — not just report on what's happening, but actively suggest improvements, surface misalignments, and propose adjustments. This moves Stratafy from a strategic intelligence platform to an autonomous strategic advisor. The implication is that the signal-to-insight-to-strategy loop should eventually close itself, with human oversight rather than human initiation. This aligns with the broader AI-native positioning and could be the defining feature that separates Stratafy from every other strategy tool.
