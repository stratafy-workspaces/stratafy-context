# Coaches validated as ideal go-to-market channel — confirmed by EO Forum feedback

## Summary
EO Forum members independently confirmed that coaches are the ideal channel for Stratafy distribution — validating the existing GTM hypothesis from the demand side.

## Description
EO Forum members independently validated the hypothesis that business coaches and strategy facilitators are the ideal distribution channel for Stratafy. This was already the planned GTM approach, but hearing it confirmed from potential end-users strengthens the conviction. Coaches bring built-in trust, recurring client relationships, and a natural entry point into strategy conversations. Combined with the insight that coaches get earlier visibility into clients through Stratafy, this creates a two-sided value proposition: coaches get a better tool and earlier insight, clients get a living strategy — and Stratafy gets distributed through an existing trusted relationship.
