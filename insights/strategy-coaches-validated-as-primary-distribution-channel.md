# Strategy coaches validated as primary distribution channel

## Summary
Rob understood immediately that coaches already do strategy definition work. Stratafy captures it in structured form — the coach gets a better deliverable, the client gets living infrastructure. Win-win distribution model validated by an external perspective.

## Description
Rob Horton immediately grasped the strategy coach channel play when Leonard explained it. Coaches already perform the initial definition work (mission, vision, values, strategies). Currently that work outputs a document or deck. With Stratafy, the same work outputs living infrastructure.

Value for the coach: their deliverable doesn't decay. They can demonstrate ongoing value through the platform. They can offer monitoring/maintenance as a recurring service.

Value for the client: they get a robustly defined strategy that is immediately ready for AI consumption, not a PDF that sits in a drawer.

This is a classic channel strategy — partner with the people who already have the relationship and do the adjacent work. The coach handles the human strategy work; Stratafy handles the infrastructure and AI layer.

Action: Formalize the coach partnership model. Define what a "Stratafy-certified coach" program looks like. Identify 3-5 coaches to pilot with beyond the current pipeline.
