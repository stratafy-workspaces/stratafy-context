# Funding & Growth had rich narrative but weak operational scaffolding

## Summary
Strategy review revealed execution detail trapped in descriptions rather than trackable initiatives/objectives. Restructured with proper hierarchy and measurables.

## Description
The Funding & Growth strategy had rich narrative content but weak operational structure — all execution detail was trapped in strategy descriptions rather than broken into trackable initiatives. The Fundraising Playbook and Seed Fundraise initiative were siblings when they should have been parent-child. No objectives existed despite clear measurable targets being defined in the strategy content. This pattern (rich strategy descriptions with thin execution scaffolding) may exist elsewhere in the tree and is worth checking during future reviews.
