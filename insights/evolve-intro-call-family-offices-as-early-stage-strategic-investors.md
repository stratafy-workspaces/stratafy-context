# Evolve intro call: family offices as early-stage strategic investors

## Summary
Family offices offer patient capital with strategic involvement and repeat investment patterns — ideal for seed stage and natural Stratafy customers for portfolio visibility.

## Description
Evolve's Carl highlighted family offices as ideal seed-stage investors due to patience, strategic involvement, and continuum of investment (cited Ethereal AI: $2M seed → $4M Series A from same family office). Family offices are "not afraid to roll their sleeves up." This validates Stratafy's value prop: family offices managing multiple investments need exactly the portfolio visibility Stratafy provides. Evolve claims ~415 family offices in network globally.
