# Fractional Executive Channel Strategy

## Summary
Stratafy can serve as infrastructure that makes fractional executives (CFOs, COOs, strategy consultants) dramatically more effective by maintaining strategic context between sessions — turning every practitioner into a distribution channel.

## Description
Emerged from Neopak lead conversation. Hamish Fraser (CEO, PE background) noted his financial team isn't strong enough and needs CFO-level capability. Key insight: Stratafy + fractional CFO is cheaper and potentially more effective than a full-time CFO hire. The platform maintains continuity between sessions, the practitioner brings strategic thinking, and the existing finance team handles operations.

This creates a three-sided value proposition:
1. For the company (Neopak): CFO-grade strategic finance at ~30-40% of full-time cost
2. For the fractional executive: Force multiplier enabling more clients at higher quality
3. For Stratafy: Channel distribution through practitioners who bring their client portfolios

Broader implication: This extends beyond CFOs to fractional COOs, strategy consultants, advisory firms (like STIR Collective already demonstrates from coaching side). Suggests a practitioner tier/mode with multi-workspace management.

Validates with two early proof points: STIR (coaching/advisory channel) and Neopak (fractional finance channel).
