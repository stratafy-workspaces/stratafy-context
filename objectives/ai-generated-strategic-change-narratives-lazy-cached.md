# AI-generated strategic change narratives (lazy-cached)

## Description
On first view of a snapshot diff, assemble structural changes + content diffs, send to LLM to generate a human-readable strategic narrative ("This week, the GTM strategy shifted focus from enterprise to mid-market after the pricing assumption was invalidated. Two new initiatives were added under Product Architecture..."). Cached on diff_analysis field. Uses distillation model (Grok 3 mini) to keep cost low. This transforms raw diffs from developer-readable to board-readable.
