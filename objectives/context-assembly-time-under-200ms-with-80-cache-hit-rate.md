# Context assembly time under 200ms with ≥ 80% cache hit rate

## Description
Pre-computed strategic context bundles served from cache in under 200ms, with at least 80% of repeat interactions within a session hitting the cache rather than triggering fresh database queries. This is the performance target that makes "strategy in the flow of work" viable — if context takes 3-5 seconds, people bypass it.
