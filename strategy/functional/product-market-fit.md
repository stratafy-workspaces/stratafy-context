## Strategic Intent

**Question answered**: Can we prove value with early adopters?

## Positioning

- Target AI-forward startups and scaling companies (10-200 employees)
- Position as "strategic infrastructure for the AI era"
- **Lead with Strategic Advisor workflow as immediate value hook** — AI that knows full context, structures thinking in real-time, and writes directly into the system
- Differentiate from OKR tools by emphasizing continuous alignment, not periodic tracking

### Leadership Team Framing (March 2026 Evolution)

The Stratafy Leadership Team provides a new lead demo hook. Instead of "let me show you our strategic operating system," demos now lead with "let me show you your Stratafy CFO." The scan becomes what the CFO does, not a standalone feature. The strategic advisor becomes Stratafy CEO-mode, not a separate concept.

All active prospect engagements should be evaluated for Leadership Team framing. Key question per prospect: would they respond better to "we'll give you an executive team" or "we'll give you strategic infrastructure"? For solo founders and early-stage teams, the executive team framing will almost always win.

The pricing reframe (R25k/month for three AI executives vs. R25k/month for a strategic operating system) is the sharpest sales tool in the arsenal.

## The Strategic Advisor Workflow

This is our core value proposition — proven in founder use:

```
1. Express fuzzy intent → 2. AI structures and challenges → 3. AI writes to system → 4. Iterate → 5. Done
```

**Outcome**: 2-3 days of strategic work in 2 hours. This is what we sell.

## The Feedback Loop (Strategy Flows Both Directions)

The Strategic Advisor workflow includes a **bottom-up feedback loop** — execution insights surface and refine strategy:

```
Insights captured → AI analyzes against strategies → Recommendations surfaced → Human approves → Strategy updated
```

**Proof point**: January 27, 2026 — 16 insights analyzed, 4 strategies updated, 1 initiative created. Total time: 5 minutes.

This operationalizes the principle "Strategy flows both directions." It's not just top-down goal-setting; execution continuously informs strategy through AI-mediated feedback.

**Customer onboarding must include**: How to capture insights, how AI surfaces implications, how to approve changes.

## Capability Requirements

### Core (PMF Phase)
- Core strategy CRUD (define, link, version strategies)
- **Alignment Scan** for foundation-strategy coherence checking
- **Feedback Loop** for insight-to-strategy refinement
- MCP integration for AI agent access to workspace context
- Strategic Advisor system prompt for grounded AI conversations
- API-first architecture for AI agent consumption

### Sequenced for Later (Phase 2)
- **Pulse** (internal execution sensing) — deliberately Phase 2
- Full Tactics layer (objectives, metrics)
- Automated drift detection

**Why Pulse is Phase 2**: Continuous alignment is a core value, but we prove the loop manually first (Strategic Advisor + Feedback Loop), then automate sensing. This prevents scope creep while still demonstrating the paradigm shift.

### Parallel Validation for A1 (De-risking)

A1 is the entire value proposition at 80% confidence. Don't wait for failure to pivot.

**Proactive mitigation**: Run parallel validation with 1-2 early customers:
- Customer A: Strategic Advisor-first workflow (primary)
- Customer B: Alignment Scan-first workflow (alternative)
- Customer C: Radar-first workflow (if capacity)

If Strategic Advisor isn't the hook by end of Q1, have validated alternative ready to deploy immediately. This reduces pivot time from months to weeks.

## Pivot Triggers

| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| 0 customers onboarded by end of March 2026 | Re-evaluate product direction, value proposition, or segment |
| 3 customers but 0% see measurable improvement | Pivot value proposition or target outcome |
| Customers consistently request X instead of Strategic Advisor | Fast-follow to build X, re-sequence roadmap |
| A1 parallel validation shows alternative outperforms | Pivot to winning workflow immediately |

## If Assumptions Prove False

**A1 (Strategic Advisor not the hook)**: Deploy pre-validated alternative (Radar-first or Alignment Scan-first) immediately.

**A2 (Wrong segment)**: Test traditional enterprises (larger budgets, slower cycles) or strategy consultants (channel partners).

**A3 (Can't ship in time)**: Reduce MVP scope further, find technical co-founder, or extend timeline.
