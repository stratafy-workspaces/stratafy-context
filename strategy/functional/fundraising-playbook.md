## GTM Network Effect Narrative — The Coach Network Pitch

### Why This Is Critical for Sequoia

Sequoia invests in network effects and distribution moats. The Global Coach Network answers the question every VC asks: "How does this scale beyond direct sales?"

Without it: "We sell strategic infrastructure to companies" — linear, high-CAC, sounds like enterprise SaaS.

With it: "Coaches are our distribution layer. Each one brings their entire portfolio. The intelligence compounds across the network." — network effects, decreasing CAC, platform economics.

### The Pitch Version

> "One coach relationship = 5-15 client deployments. Each client workspace compounds strategic intelligence. 50 coaches = 250-750 companies on the platform. We don't sell to companies — coaches sell for us, because Stratafy makes their methodology persist and their practice more valuable."

### Six Reasons This Is Sequoia-Grade

1. **1:N economics** — one sale generates 10-50 deployments. Decreasing CAC at scale. This is the advisor channel version of the VC-as-customer thesis, but more proven in the advisory world.

2. **Built-in lock-in** — client workspaces persist on Stratafy regardless of coach relationship. The data stays. Coach churn doesn't mean client churn.

3. **Intelligence moat** — cross-client pattern recognition across hundreds of workspaces. No competitor can replicate without years of accumulated strategic data. Directory without intelligence is a listing site. Intelligence without directory has no distribution. Together: defensible.

4. **City-by-city expansion playbook** — geographic scarcity mechanics, local network effects, founding partner urgency. 2-3 coaches per city creates FOMO. Feels like Uber's launch strategy.

5. **Revenue layers** — coach membership fee + client workspace subscription + revenue share. Multiple streams from one channel.

6. **Agent differentiation** — no other directory offers this. Traditional: company finds coach → emails → schedules call → coach qualifies. Stratafy: company enters URL → agent researches → workspace created → coach receives pre-researched lead with demo-ready workspace → first call is a strategic conversation. Sales cycle collapses from weeks to days.

### Positioning Decision Framework

**Must resolve by March 15** based on demo outcomes from ScaleUp (Feb 23), Limbik (Feb 24), Gert/Bev (Feb 24):

**Option B (preferred) — Validated GTM, 2-3 slides:**
Requires by April: at least 2 advisory firms actively using Stratafy with clients, directional evidence persistence layer pitch resonates, one advisor saying "I'd bring more clients onto this."

**Option A (fallback) — GTM Vision, 1 slide:**
Present as planned distribution model backed by advisory demo signals. Show the math. Acknowledge early stage. "We're validating this now — here's what we're seeing."

**Option C (last resort) — Don't feature:**
If zero advisory validation by April, keep pitch focused on direct evidence (STIR, paying customers, VC-as-customer). Save coach network for Series A story.

### Validation Questions for Every Advisory Demo

After each demo this week, specifically ask:
- "Would you bring your other clients onto this?"
- "How many clients are you working with where this would be relevant?"
- "What would it take for this to become standard in your practice?"
- "If I gave you a pre-researched workspace for a new prospect, would that change your sales process?"

If even ONE advisor says "I'd bring 5-10 clients onto this" — that's a proof point for the Sequoia pitch.

### Pitch Deck Integration

**Slide: "Distribution Through Expertise Networks"**
- The 1:N model with simple math (50 coaches × 10 clients = 500 companies)
- Visual: city-by-city expansion map (concept — the geographic playbook)
- Agent intake workflow as differentiator (URL → workspace → pre-researched lead → strategic first call)

**Moat slide integration:**
- Intelligence compounds across the network
- No competitor can replicate without the data
- Each new coach adds clients → more data → better intelligence → attracts more coaches

**CRITICAL FRAMING NOTE:**
The network story is the "why this becomes a $1B company" slide.
The proof points (STIR, ScaleUp, paying customers) are the "why you should write this check" slides.
Lead with what's real, then expand to what's possible.
Don't let vision overshadow evidence.

## Pitch Narrative Arc

1. **Problem** (2 min): Context transfer bottleneck. 90% of misalignment is poor handoffs, not bad goals. Every company deploying AI agents with zero strategic infrastructure.

2. **Solution** (2 min): Stratafy — the strategic context layer. Makes strategy persistent, queryable, and AI-accessible. Not replacing tools — connecting them.

3. **Demo** (5 min): Let investor experience it. Portfolio view → company dive → alignment scan → Strategic Advisor conversation.

4. **Traction** (2 min): STIR paying R25K/month. X additional customers/pilots. Y insights processed. Z decisions logged. Usage growing week over week.

5. **GTM** (2 min): Three channels — direct enterprise, VC portfolio deployment, Global Coach Network. The coach network is the multiplier: 1 coach = 5-15 companies. Show the math. Show the flywheel.

6. **Market** (1 min): Category timing — 18-24 month window. 88% enterprise AI adoption, zero strategic infrastructure. No incumbent in the strategic context layer.

7. **Team & Ask** (1 min): Founder-market fit (Troop to Stratafy arc). Technical co-founder [status]. $2-5M to build the team and scale the network. 50-60% engineering, 20-25% GTM.
