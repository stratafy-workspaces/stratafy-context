## Content Principles

1. **Problem education over solution promotion** ‚Äî 80% problem/insight, 20% solution
2. **Original frameworks over recycled ideas** ‚Äî Would an investor quote this?
3. **Data-backed over opinion-based** ‚Äî Every claim needs a citation
4. **Timeless foundations over trending topics** ‚Äî Valuable in 2028?
5. **Dual-use by design** ‚Äî Works in blog AND pitch meeting
6. **Generic education in Phase 0** ‚Äî No proprietary reveals before funding
7. **Human-AI collaboration** ‚Äî AI assists ideation/drafting, human approves and refines
8. **Lived experience over theory** ‚Äî Draw from Troop experience, not just research

- Bridge to Stratafy: Velocity WITH alignment, not velocity OR alignment

**Article 5: The Execution Gap Explained**
