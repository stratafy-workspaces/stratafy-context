# Advisory Firms as Customer Segment

## Description
Advisory firms as Stratafy's primary distribution channel. Core value proposition: advisory firms deploy a Stratafy Leadership Team to every client workspace. Their methodology is built into the executive personas. They don't just hand off strategy — they leave an executive team behind that maintains it between engagements.

THE ADVISORY PITCH: 'Deploy an AI executive team to every client. Your methodology, their workspace, continuous execution.' Advisory firms earn revenue share (15-20%) on every client workspace with an active Leadership Team.

WHY THIS WORKS: Advisory firms' biggest challenge is maintaining strategy engagement between sessions. The Stratafy Leadership Team solves this — a Stratafy CFO monitors financial alignment, a Stratafy COO tracks initiative progress, a Stratafy CMO maintains content pipeline — all running the advisory firm's methodology. The firm stays in the loop via cross-client dashboard without spending billable hours.

CHANNEL MECHANICS: Advisory firm onboards client → builds strategy workspace → activates Leadership Team with firm's methodology baked into executive personas → client pays subscription → firm earns revenue share → firm monitors all clients via cross-client dashboard.

This is the strongest version of the advisory pitch — it extends their engagement without extending their time, and creates recurring revenue as AI threatens their consulting model.

# Advisory Firms as Customer Segment

Target strategy advisory and consulting firms as a primary GTM channel. They onboard clients through existing strategy engagements, get continuous visibility, create lock-in, and earn revenue share. Critically: Stratafy is also their hedge against AI disruption.

## The Market Reality

Advisory firms know AI is coming for them. Strategy consulting is exactly the kind of knowledge work LLMs are getting good at. Their fear: clients use Claude directly and skip the consultant. This creates urgency.

## Value Proposition — Growth

1. **Continuous Client Visibility** — dashboard across all clients
2. **Strategic Interface** — clients interact through Stratafy, advisor stays connected
3. **Insight Generation** — pattern recognition across portfolio
4. **Client Lock-in** — strategy lives in Stratafy, switching means migration
5. **Revenue Share** — recurring revenue on every client onboarded

## Value Proposition — AI Hedge

1. **Become Infrastructure** — even if AI handles strategy work, client context lives in system they control and earn from
2. **Revenue Transition** — as consulting revenue declines, Stratafy revenue share grows
3. **Stay in Loop** — they see when clients need help, become escalation layer
4. **Lock-in Outlasts Relationship** — clients still on Stratafy even if they stop hiring for projects

## GTM Motion

- Land with advisory firm, deploy to clients post-engagement
- Revenue share on client subscriptions
- Natural onboarding: strategy session to Stratafy handoff

ScaleUp Company

**AI Hedge:** AI will change your business. Be the one who gives clients strategic AI infrastructure and earn revenue whether they need consulting or not.
