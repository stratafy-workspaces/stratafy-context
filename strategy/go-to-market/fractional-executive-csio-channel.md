# Fractional Executive & CSIO Channel

## Description
Practitioner-led GTM channel where fractional executives adopt Stratafy as their operating environment and bring it to client companies, distinct from advisory (methodology-led) and VC (portfolio-led) channels.

EVOLUTION (March 2026): The Stratafy Leadership Team IS the fractional executive, delivered as software. Real fractional executives become the human layer that complements the AI team — they handle the things the Stratafy CFO can't (negotiate with banks, sit in board meetings, read the room, make judgment calls under pressure).

LAYERED MODEL: Human fractional executive + Stratafy Leadership Team = complete coverage. The human handles relationship, judgment, and external representation. The AI handles monitoring, analysis, reporting, and coordination. Together they're more capable than either alone.

The CSIO (Chief Strategic Intelligence Officer) becomes the human who oversees the entire Stratafy Leadership Team — the person responsible for making sure the AI executives are operating correctly and escalations are handled.

This channel is now stronger, not weaker. Fractional executives who adopt Stratafy don't lose clients to AI — they become more valuable because they come with a Leadership Team built in.

# Fractional Executive & CSIO Channel

Direct-to-operator channel where fractional CFOs, COOs, and strategic operators adopt Stratafy as their operating environment, then bring it to client companies. Distinct from advisory firms (methodology-led) and VCs (portfolio-led) — this channel is practitioner-led.

## Evolution Path

1. **Entry** — Stratafy solves the context decay problem for fractional executives
2. **Evolution** — Best practitioners become AI-capable operators doing the work of 2-3 specialists
3. **Formalization** — The CSIO role emerges as a board-level position
4. **Standardization** — PE firms roll the model across portfolios

## Key Insight

Each practitioner who adopts Stratafy is a potential CSIO-in-training. The platform sale follows role adoption, not software purchase.
