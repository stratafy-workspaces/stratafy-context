# Partner Ecosystem Channel

## Description
Professional services firms — starting with HR consulting — become a distinct GTM channel by building branded Claude-powered plugins on Stratafy's infrastructure. Stratafy provides the full stack: IP ingestion and hosting, agent construction, workspace integration, and a dedicated front-end panel inside the product where the partner's brand, tools, and in-person services are resident for their clients. Partners bring domain methodology and client relationships. This channel creates compounding distribution — every partner brings their own client base, and every client engagement deepens Stratafy workspace lock-in. Distinct from the Advisory Firms channel (which sells Stratafy to advisors): this channel makes advisors builders on the platform. First target: We Are 52 (HR consulting, Cape Town) — meeting confirmed March 2026. This strategy defines the GTM motion; the Partner Plugin Ecosystem (under Product Architecture) defines the platform capability that enables it.
