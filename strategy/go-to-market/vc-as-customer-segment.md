# VC as Customer Segment

## Description
VCs as both investors in Stratafy and distribution channel to portfolio companies. The Leadership Team reframe: VCs don't just give portfolio companies 'a tool' — they give them a Stratafy Leadership Team.

THE VC PITCH: 'Sequoia-backed companies get a virtual CFO, COO, and CMO as part of post-investment support.' That's a headline that makes portfolio companies want to take the VC's money.

VC VALUE PROPOSITION:
1. Portfolio-wide strategic visibility — see strategy health across all companies via cross-workspace dashboard
2. Leadership Team deployment — give every portfolio company AI executives as part of post-investment package
3. Pattern recognition — insights from cross-portfolio strategic data
4. Active support without active time — Leadership Team maintains engagement between board meetings
5. Differentiated support offering — 'we give you AI executives' is stronger than 'we give you access to tools'

CHANNEL MECHANICS: VC invests in company → deploys Stratafy Leadership Team to portfolio company workspace → Leadership Team operates continuously → VC sees cross-portfolio dashboard → pattern insights surface → VC is more valuable to founders → more founders want VC's money.

ENGAGEMENT PATHS: Madrona (Ted Kummert, primary), Sequoia (via Pieter/Roelof), Knife Capital (local SA), future: a16z, YC network.

# VC as Customer Segment

Target VCs as a primary customer segment, not just funding sources. VCs represent an ideal GTM channel: one sale deploys Stratafy to 20+ portfolio companies.

## Value Proposition to VCs

1. **Portfolio Value Creation** — deploy strategic infrastructure to portfolio companies as differentiated value-add
2. **Portfolio Visibility** — real-time dashboard of strategic health across all companies
3. **Portfolio Engagement** — MCP access to query across portfolio, engage with company strategic agents
4. **Deal Evaluation** — assess potential investments through strategic coherence lens
5. **LP Reporting** — aggregate portfolio metrics for evidence-based updates

## GTM Motion

- Land with VC firm → they deploy to portfolio companies
- VC becomes distribution channel (one sale = many deployments)
- Network effects as portfolio companies talk and Stratafy becomes standard
- Social proof: "Sequoia uses Stratafy" validates for entire market

## Architecture Fit

- VC Partner = Strategic Interface (portfolio-wide view)
- Portfolio CEO = Strategic Advisor (company-specific)
- Role-Aware Strategic Intelligence supports this natively
