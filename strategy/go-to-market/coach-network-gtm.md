# Coach Network GTM

## Description
Go-to-market strategy targeting advisory firms and certified coaches across major strategy methodology networks (Scaling Up, EOS, OKRs, Balanced Scorecard, 4DX) as Stratafy's primary distribution channel.

EVOLUTION (March 2026): With the Stratafy Leadership Team, coaches become the human configuration layer for AI executives. They set up client workspaces, customize executive personas with their methodology, quality-check AI executives' work, and handle the human judgment calls the AI can't. The coach doesn't compete with the Stratafy CMO — the coach is the person who makes sure the Stratafy CMO reflects the right methodology.

This makes coaches MORE valuable, not less. They're the ones who make the AI team work correctly. Their methodology becomes the instruction set for a Stratafy Leadership Team that operates continuously between coaching sessions.

## Content
## Strategic Intent

**Question answered**: Can methodology coaches become Stratafy's primary distribution channel?

**Time Horizon**: Medium-term (12-24 months)

**Strategic Theme**: Position Stratafy as the persistence and intelligence layer for every major strategy methodology, using coaches as the distribution multiplier.

## Positioning

**Public narrative** — Stratafy is additive to your methodology. It makes your methodology persist between sessions, gives you cross-portfolio visibility, and prepares your clients for AI governance.

**Internal trajectory** — Subsumption. Over 12-24 months, Stratafy becomes the system of record while methodologies become vocabulary within the platform.

## Distribution Model

One coach equals many client deployments. Each coach relationship generates 3-5+ client workspace subscriptions, creating a channel multiplier effect that scales without proportional sales effort.

## Revenue Model

**Coaches** get premium features: portfolio analytics, branded reports, cross-client intelligence, and enhanced directory profiles.

**Clients** get workspace subscriptions sourced through their coach relationship.

Revenue share mechanics align coach incentives with platform adoption.

## Key Advantage

Leonard has warm relationships with coaches across Scaling Up and other methodology networks. This is an existing distribution advantage to activate — not a cold outreach play.
