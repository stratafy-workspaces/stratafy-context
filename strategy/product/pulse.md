# Pulse

## Description
Continuous monitoring system that tracks strategic alignment and execution health through regular AI-powered check-ins with team members. Pulse is a feed — asynchronous, individual, lightweight, and recurring. It detects drift early, keeps partners and leadership embedded in client relationships week to week, and generates trend data over the life of an engagement. Distinct from InsightSync (which is a session): Pulse monitors, InsightSync facilitates.

## Content
# Pulse

## Purpose and Value

Pulse transforms strategy execution from periodic assessment to continuous monitoring, providing real-time visibility into organisational alignment and early warning for strategic drift before it impacts performance.

Pulse is a **monitoring tool** — not a facilitation tool. It runs asynchronously, with individuals, on a recurring schedule. It does not bring people together to work through a question (that is InsightSync). It watches for drift, surfaces signals early, and keeps the right people informed without requiring a meeting.

### The Problem It Solves

**Before Pulse:**
- Strategic alignment only measured quarterly or annually
- Execution problems discovered too late to prevent impact
- No early warning system for strategic drift
- Leadership operates blindly between formal reviews
- Partners disengage from client relationships between milestone sessions

**After Pulse:**
- Weekly visibility into alignment trends and changes
- Early identification of execution problems (weeks vs. months)
- Proactive intervention prevents strategic drift
- Partners stay resident in client relationships through continuous data
- Data-driven decision making with current, not stale, information

## How It Works

### Continuous Monitoring Process

**1. Weekly AI Check-ins**
- Brief Friday conversations with team members (3–5 minutes)
- AI-powered dialogue focused on strategic understanding and alignment
- Adaptive questioning based on previous responses and trends
- Non-intrusive integration into weekly workflows — should feel like a brief reflection, not a survey

**2. Dynamic Trend Tracking**
- Week-over-week alignment measurement with trend visualisation
- Component analysis tracking Understanding (comprehension), Initiative Alignment (work connection), and Purpose Connection (personal meaning)
- Statistical trend analysis and pattern recognition
- Confidence intervals and reliability scoring

**3. Early Warning System**
- Automatic identification of strategic drift indicators
- Alert thresholds for significant alignment degradation
- Predictive modelling for potential execution problems
- Escalation protocols for leadership or partner intervention

**4. Adaptive Guidance**
- AI-generated recommendations for reinforcing weakening alignment areas
- Personalised suggestions based on individual and team patterns
- Success pattern recognition and best practice sharing

## When to Use Pulse

Pulse is a **recurring monitoring feed** — deployed at the start of an execution phase and run continuously throughout. It is not a one-time diagnostic, not a session, and not triggered by specific events.

**Appropriate deployment moments:**
- Start of execution following a strategy or roadmap delivery
- During any initiative phase where team alignment is critical
- Post-merger integration monitoring
- Strategic pivot tracking — how quickly is the team adapting?
- Culture alignment monitoring over time
- Anywhere leadership needs ongoing visibility without requiring scheduled reviews

**The defining characteristic: Pulse is always running. It is the heartbeat of an engagement, not a milestone in it.**

## Partner Plugin Application — We Are 52

Pulse is the **monitoring layer** of the We Are 52 partner engagement model. For 52, this means:

**Ongoing Engagement Monitoring** — once a client has received the People Strategy Roadmap, Pulse runs weekly check-ins with key team members. Questions are grounded in 52's specific diagnostic outputs — the four people pillars, the quick wins, the identified risks. Not generic alignment questions. Questions like: "The roadmap identified [Pillar X] as your top priority — how is your team tracking against that this week?"

**Partner Residency Tool** — Pulse is what keeps We Are 52 embedded in a client relationship between formal sessions. Without it, 52 is only present at milestone moments. With it, they have a live data feed from the client's organisation — which means every call with the client starts from current information, not from memory of the last meeting.

**Trend Data as Proof of Impact** — Pulse trend data is the evidence 52 needs to demonstrate ROI. "Alignment on the Talent Engine pillar has improved from 42% to 67% over 8 weeks" is a concrete outcome, not a qualitative claim. This is a commercial differentiator for 52 that no traditional consulting firm can match.

**Commercial Model** — Pulse creates a recurring engagement reason. Clients don't just pay for the diagnostic and the roadmap — they pay for continuous monitoring through the execution phase. This changes 52's commercial model from project-based to retainer-based.

This makes Pulse the **session layer** complement to InsightSync — InsightSync generates intelligence from facilitated conversations, Pulse monitors alignment over time between those conversations.

## Key Capabilities

### Trend Analysis and Measurement
- **Primary Output**: Weekly alignment scores with trend visualisation
- **Component Tracking**: Understanding, Initiative Alignment, and Purpose Connection measured separately
- **Improvement Validation**: Confirms whether strategic interventions are working

- **Individual Tracking**: Person-specific alignment patterns and improvement areas

- **Trend Visualisation**: Executive-level alignment improvement over time
- **Alert System**: Notifications for significant changes or concerning trends
- **Intervention Guidance**: Specific recommendations for addressing alignment gaps
- **Progress Validation**: Confirmation that strategic initiatives are improving alignment

## Relationship to Other Tools

**Pulse vs InsightSync**: Complementary, not competing. Pulse is a feed — asynchronous, individual, lightweight, recurring. InsightSync is a session — synchronous, multi-participant, structured, ad hoc. InsightSync generates intelligence from conversations. Pulse monitors alignment over time. Together they form the complete engagement arc for partner firms: intake session (InsightSync) → roadmap delivery → readiness check (InsightSync) → ongoing monitoring (Pulse).

**Pulse + Stratafy Workspace**: Pulse trend data flows into the workspace as live insights and metrics — alignment scores update, risk flags are raised, and leadership dashboards reflect current reality. The workspace is the integration layer that connects Pulse's monitoring feed to the full strategic picture.
