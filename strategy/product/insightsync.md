# InsightSync

## Purpose and Value

InsightSync transforms strategic conversations from ad-hoc discussions into systematic intelligence generation. It creates organisational memory while facilitating consensus building and strategic decision-making through AI-powered collaborative intelligence.

InsightSync is a **conversation tool** — not a monitoring tool. It is deployed whenever a group needs to work through something together: building shared understanding, surfacing divergence, making decisions, or generating structured input. It runs synchronously, with multiple participants, and produces outputs that land directly in the Stratafy workspace as live entities.

### The Problem It Solves

**Before InsightSync:**
- Strategic conversations lack structure and often don't reach consensus
- Valuable insights from discussions are lost or forgotten
- No systematic way to capture and build on organisational strategic thinking
- Strategic decisions made without comprehensive team input
- Divergence between leaders goes undetected until it causes execution failure

**After InsightSync:**
- Structured, AI-facilitated conversations that build consensus systematically
- Organisational memory that preserves strategic context and decision-making rationale
- Real-time pattern recognition surfaces hidden alignment gaps and divergence
- Strategic intelligence that compounds through accumulated organisational conversations

## When to Use InsightSync

InsightSync is an **ad hoc tool** — deployed across the organisation whenever a strategic question requires structured group input. It is not a recurring monitor (that is Pulse). It is a facilitated session that produces structured intelligence.

**Common deployment moments:**
- Diagnostic intake — replacing manual interviews at the start of a consulting engagement
- Pre-execution readiness — checking stakeholder alignment before an initiative launches
- Change readiness — before a strategic pivot, restructure, or major initiative
- Initiative kickoffs — aligning cross-functional teams on goals, assumptions, and ownership
- Culture resets — surfacing alignment and divergence on values and ways of working
- Post-merger integration — identifying friction between teams with different operating norms
- Decision forums — structured group input before high-stakes decisions are finalised
- Strategic reviews — periodic alignment checks with leadership teams

The defining characteristic: **InsightSync is always a session. It brings people together to produce structured intelligence from a conversation.**

## Partner Plugin Application — We Are 52

InsightSync is the primary vehicle for partner firms to productise their intake and facilitation methodology on the Stratafy platform. For We Are 52, this means:

**Discovery Diagnostic Intake Session** — replaces manual interviews with an AI-facilitated multi-stakeholder session. 3–5 leadership participants respond to 52's proprietary question framework simultaneously. Claude surfaces where executives agree and where they diverge. Divergence is a diagnosis in itself — a CEO and COO describing different "biggest people challenges" is a finding, not a discrepancy to smooth over. Output feeds the Discovery Diagnostic roadmap directly.

**Pre-Execution Readiness Session** — run one week after roadmap delivery. Uses InsightSync with a custom readiness template to confirm whether leadership has shared understanding of priorities before execution begins. Surfaces misalignment before momentum dies. This is a billable touchpoint 52 currently does not have a structured product for.

**Ad Hoc Facilitation** — any point in the engagement where a strategic question requires structured group input: culture reset conversations, initiative kickoff alignment, change readiness at inflection points. Each session is deployable in minutes using saved templates, and all outputs land in the client's Stratafy workspace.

This makes InsightSync the **session layer** of the partner engagement model — paired with Pulse (the monitoring layer) to form a complete AI-powered engagement arc.

## Relationship to Other Tools

**InsightSync vs Pulse**: These are complementary, not competing. InsightSync is a session — synchronous, multi-participant, structured, ad hoc. Pulse is a feed — asynchronous, individual, lightweight, recurring. InsightSync generates intelligence from conversations. Pulse monitors alignment over time. Together they form the full engagement arc for partner firms: intake session → readiness check → ongoing monitoring.

**InsightSync + Stratafy Workspace**: Every InsightSync session produces outputs that land directly in the workspace as live entities — insights, decisions, risks, and initiatives. This is the core value of the partner plugin model: 52's facilitated conversations produce structured Stratafy data, not PDF reports.
