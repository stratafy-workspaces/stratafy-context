# Stratafy Leadership Team

## Description
First-party AI executive team delivered as Claude Cowork plugins — a Stratafy CFO, COO, CMO, and more — that operate continuously inside a company's workspace. Each executive plugin is powered by the Role-Aware Strategic Intelligence context layer, which scopes what the plugin sees, how it speaks, what it can do, and what it captures back into the strategic system. This is the product layer that makes the strategic operating system tangible. Every GTM channel now pitches this: 'R25k/month for three AI executives' beats 'R25k/month for a strategic operating system.' Current state: Finance Director (v0.3.0), Team Partner (v0.1.0), Coach (v1.3.0). Next: COO, CMO, CEO-mode, CHRO (potential We Are 52 co-build).

# Stratafy Leadership Team

## Strategic Intent

**Question answered**: How do we deliver the strategic operating system as a product people immediately understand and want?

**Answer**: We deliver it as AI executives — a CFO, COO, CMO — that work inside the company's workspace, grounded in their strategy, and operate continuously between human sessions.

**Time Horizon**: Q1 2026 → Q4 2026 (medium-term, phased rollout)

**Strategic Theme**: The Leadership Team is the product layer that makes the strategic operating system tangible. Infrastructure buyers discover it through architecture. Everyone else discovers it through "I need a CFO."

---

## Why This Matters Now

The biggest friction in selling Stratafy is abstraction. "Strategic operating system" requires explanation. "AI Finance Director that monitors your spend against your strategy" does not.

| Framing | Audience | Conversion Signal |
|---------|----------|-------------------|
| Strategic Operating System | CTOs, technical founders | Slow — requires education |
| Control Plane for AI | AI-native orgs | Medium — niche but resonant |
| Strategic Context Layer | Developers, platform thinkers | Slow — developer-first positioning |
| **AI Leadership Team** | **Solo founders, SMEs, scaling teams** | **Fast — immediate need recognition** |

The Leadership Team framing doesn't replace the others — it adds a product-level entry point that makes the infrastructure framings tangible. Some buyers enter through "I need a CFO" and discover the strategic operating system underneath.

**The pricing reframe**: R25k/month for three AI executives vs. R25k/month for a strategic operating system. Same product. Radically different buyer response.

### Architecture Pattern (Proven)

Every executive plugin follows a consistent structure:

1. **Role persona** with adaptive voice (director-level vs team member)
2. **Command-based workflows** mapped to the role's cadence (daily, weekly, quarterly)
3. **Skills** that teach Claude the domain (financial intelligence, strategic literacy, etc.)
4. **Authority boundaries** — what the executive can decide vs must escalate
5. **MCP tool access** scoped to the role's domain (all share Stratafy MCP, 169 tools)
6. **Activity logging** for team rhythm tracking and adoption metrics

### Key Differentiator

Every executive shares the **same strategy tree** as source of truth. The CFO's financial scan references the same strategies the COO tracks initiatives against. The CMO's content pipeline aligns to the same positioning the CEO-mode advisor reviews. This is not five separate AI tools — it's one strategic intelligence layer with role-specific interfaces.

## The Executive Roster

### Phase 1: Now (Q1 2026) — Foundation

**Stratafy Finance Director (stratafy-finance v0.3.0)**
- COA design aligned to strategy
- Budget mapping: connect every dollar to strategic intent
- Financial alignment scans (structural, P&L, balance sheet)
- Quarterly financial reviews with strategic context
- Investor prep: narratives, metrics, data room
- Pricing model design and analysis
- Authority: financial proposals, COA changes, alignment insights. Escalates: cash flow concerns, material variance, fundraising timing.

**Stratafy Team Partner (stratafy-team v0.1.0)**
- Daily rhythm: /lets-go (start day), /call-it-a-day (end day)
- Weekly rhythm: /start-the-week (Monday briefing), /week-in-review (Friday wrap)
- /catch-me-up: what changed while you were away
- /why-are-we-doing-this: trace any work to strategic purpose
- /coach-me: strategic coaching through questions
- /advise-me: direct advice with trade-offs
- Role-adaptive: engineering, sales, marketing, ops, leadership, finance — same data, different lens
- Authority: surface context, log insights, create decisions. Cannot change priorities.

### Phase 2: Q2 2026 — Core Team

- Initiative tracking and health monitoring
- OKR management and scoring
- Operational rhythm: weekly initiative reviews, monthly portfolio health
- Resource allocation visibility
- Cross-initiative dependency tracking
- Bottleneck detection and escalation
- Authority: initiative status updates, operational insights, rhythm enforcement. Escalates: resource conflicts, strategy pivots.

**Stratafy CMO (planned)**
- Content pipeline management aligned to positioning
- SEO strategy execution and monitoring
- Brand voice governance
- Channel performance tracking
- Competitive positioning updates
- Content calendar and publishing rhythm
- Authority: content decisions, channel tactics, positioning insights. Escalates: brand pivots, major positioning shifts.

### Phase 3: Q3 2026 — Leadership Layer

**Stratafy CEO-mode / Strategic Advisor (planned)**
- The existing scan + advisor functionality wrapped as executive persona
- Strategic alignment reviews
- Board preparation and reporting
- Assumption and risk monitoring
- Decision facilitation with strategic context
- Cross-executive coordination (sees what CFO, COO, CMO are surfacing)

**Stratafy CHRO (planned — potential We Are 52 co-build)**

- **Partner opportunity**: We Are 52 brings 52 years of HR methodology. This becomes the reference implementation for the Partner Plugin Ecosystem — a third-party firm building a branded executive on Stratafy infrastructure.

### Phase 4: Post-Seed — Specialist Executives

## Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Executive plugins feel shallow vs. real human executives | Medium | High | Deep domain skills, clear authority boundaries, honest about what AI can't do |
| Context window limits degrade quality with multiple plugins active | Medium | Medium | Scoped MCP access per plugin, workspace snapshots, progressive loading |
| Pricing too high for early-stage companies | Medium | Medium | Single executive tier at R8-12k, prove ROI at finance director level first |
| Partner co-build (We Are 52) stalls or fails | Low-Medium | Medium | CHRO is Phase 3 — not critical path. First-party fallback possible. |

## Strategic Constraints

- Each executive plugin MUST share the same strategy tree — no siloed intelligence
- Authority boundaries MUST be explicit — AI executives recommend, humans decide
- The coach plugin (stratafy-cowork) remains the methodology layer — executive plugins are the execution layer
- Plugin quality > plugin quantity — ship fewer, deeper executives rather than many shallow ones
- First-party executives (CFO, COO, CMO) must be proven before inviting partner-built executives
