# Strategic Intelligence

The Cortex layer capabilities that make organizations smarter over time.

## Four Intelligence Capabilities

1. **Insights** — capture what's being learned
2. **Decisions** — capture what's being chosen
3. **Radar** — scan the external environment through your strategic lens
4. **Synthesis** — connect them all

## The Learning Loop

Together they form the learning loop — the organization's capacity to sense internally, scan externally, decide, and improve continuously. This is the intelligence that compounds and creates the cold-start moat.
