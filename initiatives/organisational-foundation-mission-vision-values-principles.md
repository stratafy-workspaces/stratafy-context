## Overview

The identity layer defining who the organisation is and what it stands for. Mission, vision, values, beliefs, and principles each have list and detail views, with MVV alignment analytics measuring how well operational reality reflects stated identity.

## Scope

- **Mission & Vision**: Foundational statements with rich editing and versioning
- **Values**: Core organisational values with descriptions and behavioural indicators
- **Beliefs**: Underlying assumptions about the world that shape strategy
- **Principles**: Operating guidelines that translate values into daily decisions
- **MVV Alignment Analytics**: Measure coherence between stated identity and operational reality across the strategy tree

## Expected Outcomes

- Provide the "why" anchor that grounds all strategic decisions
- Enable AI to reference organisational identity when advising on strategy
- Surface misalignment between what the organisation says and what it does
- Create institutional memory of foundational commitments that persists through leadership changes
