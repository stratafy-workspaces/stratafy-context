## Overview

Organisational design layer connecting team structure to strategy ownership and permissions. Teams, positions, and org proposals enable leadership to model and evolve their organisational architecture alongside their strategy.

## Scope

- **Teams & Members**: Team creation and membership management with MCP tools
- **Positions**: Role definitions with monthly cost and burn rate calculations
- **Org Proposals**: Proposed organisational changes with reports-to hierarchy modelling
- **Badge Filters**: Visual filtering and categorisation of organisational elements
- **Bulk MCP Creation**: Efficient bulk operations for rapid org structure setup
- Connection between team structure, strategy ownership, and permission scoping

## Expected Outcomes

- Enable leadership to see how organisational structure maps to strategic ownership
- Provide cost modelling for organisational design decisions
- Support org change proposals with before/after hierarchy visualisation
- Connect permissions and access control to organisational position
