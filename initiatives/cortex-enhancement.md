## Overview

Integrate basic Cortex functionality into the strategy system — enabling alignment checks between components, gap detection across the strategy tree, and AI-powered analysis with actionable suggestions.

## Scope

### Alignment Checks
- Verify alignment between initiatives and mission/vision
- Cross-reference objectives with strategy intent
- Surface disconnected or orphaned strategy elements

### Gap Detection
- Identify missing connections in the strategy tree
- Surface strategies without supporting initiatives
- Detect objectives without measurable outcomes

### On-Demand Reports
- AI-generated analysis of strategy coherence
- Recommendations for strengthening alignment
- Exportable summaries for leadership review

### AI Suggestions
- Proactive recommendations based on strategy structure
- "You might be missing..." guidance based on common patterns
- Contextual suggestions during strategy editing

## Expected Outcomes
- Strategy coherence becomes measurable and improvable
- Gaps surfaced automatically rather than through manual review
- AI suggestions accelerate strategy quality improvement
