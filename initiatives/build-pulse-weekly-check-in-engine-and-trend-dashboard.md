## Overview

The Pulse Weekly Check-in Engine provides AI-driven Friday conversations (3-5 minutes per person) that measure strategic alignment across three dimensions, with leadership dashboards showing trends and actionable intervention guidance.

## Scope

### Weekly Check-in Engine
- AI-driven 3-5 minute Friday conversations per team member
- Adaptive questioning based on previous responses and current strategic focus
- Natural language dialogue, not robotic surveys

### Three-Dimension Measurement
- **Understanding**: Strategy comprehension depth
- **Initiative Alignment**: Work-to-strategy connection strength
- **Purpose Connection**: Personal meaning and motivation

### Trend Analysis
- Week-over-week tracking per individual and team
- Statistical trend analysis with confidence intervals
- Significant change detection and alerting

### Leadership Dashboard
- Alignment trends across teams and dimensions
- Alert system for significant changes
- Intervention guidance with specific recommendations

## Expected Outcomes
- Leaders gain real-time visibility into strategic alignment across the organisation
- Degradation detected in days rather than quarters
- Specific intervention recommendations replace vague "alignment feels off" concerns
