## Overview

Design a structured AI literacy workshop framework that coaches can deliver to their clients, positioning Stratafy as the tooling backbone. This turns the AI literacy gap into a revenue opportunity for coaches while driving platform adoption.

## Scope

### Workshop Design
- Modular workshop format adaptable to different methodology frameworks (Scaling Up, EOS, OKRs)
- Coach-led delivery model where the coach is the human configuration layer for AI executives
- Hands-on Stratafy workspace exercises demonstrating the Leadership Team concept

### Coach Value Proposition
- Coaches become MORE valuable as the ones who make AI teams work correctly
- Their methodology becomes the instruction set for the Stratafy Leadership Team
- Revenue opportunity: coaches charge for workshops, Stratafy gains adoption

### Deliverables
- Workshop curriculum and facilitator guide
- Participant workbook with Stratafy workspace exercises
- Coach certification pathway outline

## Expected Outcomes

- Scalable onboarding mechanism through coach network
- Coach-driven platform adoption without direct sales effort
- AI literacy positioned as coach value-add, not threat
- Template for methodology-specific workshop variants
