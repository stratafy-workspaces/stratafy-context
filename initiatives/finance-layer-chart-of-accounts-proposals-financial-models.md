## Overview

Complete financial management layer connecting organisational spend to strategic intent. Includes Chart of Accounts with hierarchical trees, financial proposals with budget line items, strategy-to-account mappings, and an AI-driven financial model engine.

## Scope

- **Chart of Accounts**: Hierarchical account trees with animated transitions and COA Health Check
- **Financial Proposals**: Budget line items linked to strategies and accounts
- **Strategy-Account Mappings**: Connect every dollar of spend to strategic intent
- **Financial Model Engine**: AI-driven modelling with KaTeX rendering and natural language editing via the AI chat panel
- Full CRUD across all finance entities with MCP tool support

## Expected Outcomes

- Enable leadership to see exactly how budget allocation maps to strategic priorities
- Provide AI-assisted financial modelling that non-finance users can operate conversationally
- Create the financial accountability layer that grounds strategy in resource reality
- Deliver COA health diagnostics that surface structural issues in financial planning
