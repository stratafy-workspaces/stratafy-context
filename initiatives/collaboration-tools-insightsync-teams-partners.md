## Overview

Collaboration infrastructure that transforms Stratafy from a planning tool into a working environment. InsightSync provides structured workshop sessions, team management handles workspace membership, and Partners enables external collaboration.

## Scope

- **InsightSync**: Structured workshop sessions with customisable prompts, participant management, response collection, and AI-powered analysis of participant inputs
- **Teams**: Workspace membership management, role assignment, and team-based permissions
- **Partners**: External collaboration framework allowing advisors, consultants, and stakeholders to participate in strategic work without full workspace access

## Expected Outcomes

- Enable distributed strategic thinking through structured async workshops
- Move from solo planning to collaborative strategy development
- Provide advisory firms with a repeatable client engagement framework
- Create feedback loops where participant inputs generate signals and insights automatically
