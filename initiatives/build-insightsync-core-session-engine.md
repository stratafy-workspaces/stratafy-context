## Overview

The core InsightSync session engine provides multi-modal strategic conversation facilitation with real-time AI pattern recognition. This is the foundation all InsightSync use cases run on — the quality of conversation directly drives both participation and insight quality.

## Scope

### Multi-Modal Access
- QR code, web, mobile, Slack/Teams entry points
- Seamless join experience across devices

### Real-Time AI Facilitation
- Live pattern recognition during conversations
- Theme identification and consensus tracking
- Divergence surfacing when perspectives differ
- Adaptive questioning based on conversation flow

### Conversation Quality
- Natural dialogue feel, not robotic survey
- Engagement-driven design that maximizes both participation and insight quality

## Expected Outcomes
- Foundation engine supporting all three InsightSync use cases
- Natural conversation quality that drives sustained engagement
- Real-time pattern recognition producing actionable intelligence from every session
