## Overview

Build a multi-agent organization where AI agents coordinate through Linear, operating from shared strategic context via Stratafy. Leonard remains the sole human, approving Type 1 decisions while agents handle execution autonomously.

## Scope

This initiative is the internal dogfooding engine for the Stratafy Leadership Team. Every agent built internally becomes a pre-built executive persona for customers:

- **Chief of Staff agent** → Stratafy COO prototype
- **Content Pipeline agent** → Stratafy CMO prototype
- **Financial management workflows** → Stratafy CFO prototype
- **CRM automation** → feeds Stratafy COO coordination

## Objectives

1. **Create Capacity** — Multiply founder leverage. Target: 10-20 hours/week reclaimed
2. **Prove the Leadership Team** — Stress-test each executive persona before customer release
3. **Generate Insights** — Document learnings, feed into prompt templates and authority boundaries
4. **Create Case Studies** — Build pitch material showing a solo founder running a company with Stratafy Leadership Team

## Expected Outcomes

- Validated executive personas ready for customer deployment
- Hours reclaimed metrics demonstrating AI leverage
- Published case study before Sequoia pitch
- Product improvements driven by internal usage insights
