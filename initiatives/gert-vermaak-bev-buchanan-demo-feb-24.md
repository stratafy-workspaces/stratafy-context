# Gert Vermaak + Bev Buchanan Demo — Feb 24

## Description
In-person demo in Stellenbosch, Tuesday Feb 24. Gert Vermaak (Viridis AFC) is bringing Bev Buchanan (Principal at GlobeScan) as a prospective client for the strategy consulting firm Gert wants to build on Stratafy.

STRATEGIC SIGNIFICANCE: This is the first live test of the advisory-firm-as-distribution thesis. Gert isn't just a customer — he's a channel partner building his practice on Stratafy. Bev is his first client prospect. If this converts, it validates that consultants can sell through Stratafy, not just to Stratafy.

BEV'S CONTEXT: 15+ years in research and strategic advisory at GlobeScan (sustainability/stakeholder engagement consultancy). Expert in stakeholder relationships, organizational culture, facilitation. Based in Cape Town. Speaks English and Afrikaans. Stellenbosch University alumna. Works with major African corporates (Safaricom, Distell, Old Mutual, Exxaro).

POSITIONING: Lead with the pain Bev already knows — lossy context transfer between advisory insight and client execution. GlobeScan generates research insights that clients are supposed to act on. Show how Stratafy makes that transfer persistent. The alignment scorecard concept should resonate with her facilitation background.

DUAL PLAY:
1. Convert Bev/GlobeScan as a client workspace (through Gert's practice)
2. Validate Gert as a functioning channel partner

DEMO APPROACH: Use STIR workspace as live proof. Let Gert co-present where possible — he needs to learn how to sell this.

SUCCESS CRITERIA:
- Bev sees clear value for her advisory work
- Gert demonstrates confidence presenting the platform
- Follow-up engagement agreed (pilot, workspace setup, or next meeting)
