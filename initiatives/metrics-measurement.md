## Overview

The measurement layer that grounds strategic intent in observable reality. Metrics with full CRUD, historical value recording, and trend analysis — connected to objectives and strategies to answer "are we on track?"

## Scope

- Full CRUD for metrics with type classification and target values
- Historical value recording with timestamps for trend tracking
- Trend analysis and visualisation of metric progression over time
- Linkage to objectives and strategies for contextual measurement
- MCP tools for programmatic metric recording and querying

## Expected Outcomes

- Provide objective, data-driven answers to "are we making progress?"
- Enable automated metric tracking through MCP integrations
- Create the feedback loop between strategic intent and measurable outcomes
- Surface early warning signals when metrics diverge from targets
