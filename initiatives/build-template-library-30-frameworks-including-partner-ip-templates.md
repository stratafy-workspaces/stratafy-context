## Overview

A comprehensive template library powering InsightSync sessions across three categories, with a critical partner template framework that enables advisory firms to encode their IP as custom template sets.

## Scope

### Foundation Templates (8)
- Core values discovery, mission alignment, vision crafting, strategic priority alignment

### Operational Templates (12)
- Meeting effectiveness, change readiness, digital transformation, process improvement

### Performance Templates (10)
- Goal setting/OKR, skills gap analysis, risk identification, crisis response

### Partner Template Framework
- Partner IP encoded as custom template sets
- First partner: We Are 52 Discovery Diagnostic — methodology-grounded questions, not generic
- Templates support workspace entity output (strategies, objectives, risks, decisions pushed to live workspace)

## Key Requirement
All templates must output to workspace entities, not PDFs. The conversation produces live strategic objects, not static documents.

## Expected Outcomes
- 30+ ready-to-use templates covering common strategic conversation needs
- Partner template framework enables advisory firm IP integration
- Workspace entity output creates immediate value from every session
