## Overview

The core system for defining and structuring strategy. A hierarchy of strategies, initiatives, objectives, and plans with full CRUD, rich detail views, and content editing. Key Priorities surface leadership's top concerns to influence AI context across the workspace.

## Scope

- **Strategies**: Top-level strategic directions with descriptions, content, and ownership
- **Initiatives**: Executable programmes of work linked to strategies with status tracking
- **Objectives**: Measurable goals with OKR scoring and key results
- **Plans**: Tactical execution documents connected to initiatives
- **Key Priorities**: First-class entity with drag-to-reorder dashboard and MCP tools — leadership's top 3-5 current priorities that shape AI context workspace-wide
- Full content editing with markdown support and version history

## Expected Outcomes

- Provide leadership with a clear answer to "what are we doing and why?"
- Build connective tissue between high-level intent and operational execution
- Enable AI to understand strategic context through the priority hierarchy
- Create the structural backbone that all other product layers reference
