## Overview

A system that synthesizes patterns across decisions, assumptions, and outcomes to generate organizational wisdom over time. When strategies succeed or fail, when assumptions validate or invalidate, when decisions play out — the system learns and surfaces patterns that improve future judgment. This is what makes the flywheel claim real.

## Scope

### Outcome Tracking
- Decision outcomes: what happened vs what we expected
- Pattern recognition across assumption validations/invalidations
- Strategy success/failure post-mortems with causal analysis

### Synthesis & Reporting
- "Here's what we learned" periodic synthesis reports
- Recommendation quality improvement based on historical accuracy
- Organizational wisdom accumulation over time

### Learning Loop
- System judgment improves with every decision cycle
- Cold-start moat deepens as data accumulates
- Historical patterns inform future recommendations

## Dependencies
- Decision Log, Assumptions, Insights, and Alignment Scan all active
- Sufficient data: 3-6 months minimum of meaningful usage
- Requires meaningful customer usage patterns before building

## Expected Outcomes
- Stratafy demonstrably gets smarter over time, not just accumulates data
- Cold-start moat deepens with every cycle of decisions and outcomes
- Recommendation quality measurably improves based on historical accuracy
