## Purpose

Build the coach-specific feature set that makes Stratafy indispensable for advisory practices. These features make coaches more valuable, not less — turning them from quarterly facilitators into continuous strategic advisors.

## Core Features

**Portfolio Dashboard** — See strategic health across all clients at a glance, spot drift, identify at-risk accounts. The single-pane view that no coach currently has.

**Cross-Client Intelligence** — Surface patterns across the portfolio. Examples: 3 clients invalidated the same market assumption, common risk patterns by industry, methodology adoption curves.

**Branded Reports** — White-label alignment scans, strategic health reports, and investor updates with the coach's branding. Makes the coach look more professional and data-driven.

**Client Comparison** — Benchmark engagement depth, assumption validation rates, and decision velocity across clients. Helps coaches identify which clients need more attention.

**Session Prep Auto-Brief** — Before each coaching session, auto-generate a brief of what changed since last session: new decisions, risk movements, assumption updates, metric trends. Collapses prep time from hours to minutes.
