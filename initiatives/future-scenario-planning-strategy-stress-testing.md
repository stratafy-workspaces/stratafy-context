## Overview

Forward-looking capability that stress-tests strategy against alternative futures. "What happens if?" analysis that models the impact of external shocks and internal changes on the strategy tree, enabling boards and investors to trust the strategic plan.

## Scope

### Scenario Definition
- Competitor acquisition, customer loss, market shift, headcount reduction
- Custom scenarios tailored to workspace context

### Analysis Capabilities
- Cascade analysis: if X happens, which strategies/initiatives survive, which collapse?
- Strategy resilience scoring across multiple futures
- Assumption sensitivity: which assumptions, if wrong, cause the most damage?
- Resource reallocation modeling: given scenario X, where should capacity shift?

### Use Cases
- Board meetings: "we've stress-tested against 5 scenarios, here's our resilience"
- Investor confidence: demonstrates strategic maturity beyond optimistic planning
- Founder decision-making: quantifies trade-offs before committing resources
- Advisory firms: scenario workshops as premium deliverable

## Architecture
- Sits in Cortex alongside the three scans
- Uses strategy tree, assumptions, risks, and organigram as inputs
- AI-powered analysis with human review

## Expected Outcomes
- Strategy robustness becomes measurable across alternative futures
- Boards and investors gain confidence in strategic maturity
- Advisory firms offer scenario workshops as a premium service
