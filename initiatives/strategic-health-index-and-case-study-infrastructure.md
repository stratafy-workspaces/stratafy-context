## Content
## Overview

Instrument and track flywheel metrics from day one for Stratafy and every customer. This data becomes case studies for investors and sales, proving the flywheel is real with cohort curves and acceleration data.

## Scope

### Phase 1 — Instrumentation (Now)
- Context Depth Score: foundation completeness, strategy count, insight count, word counts
- Agent Activity: MCP sessions, context retrievals, unique clients
- Value Created: insights generated, insights per week, sources
- Embedding Factors: time on platform, active users, integrations
- Weekly snapshots per workspace, backfill-able

### Phase 2 — Internal Tracking (Feb-Mar)
- Weekly export/query of Stratafy's own flywheel metrics
- Manual tracking spreadsheet for early customers
- Define cohort analysis for investor presentations

### Phase 3 — Customer Dashboard (Q2)
- Flywheel dashboard page with four-quadrant visualization
- Velocity trend over time
- Milestone celebrations and engagement nudges

### Phase 4 — Case Study Automation (Q2-Q3)
- Auto-generate case study snapshots
- Month 1 vs Month 6 comparisons per company
- Anonymized cohort curves for pitch deck

## Expected Outcomes
- Investors see proof the flywheel works via cohort data
- Customers visualize their own strategic acceleration
- Sales gains automated case studies from real usage patterns
- Product team understands what drives retention
