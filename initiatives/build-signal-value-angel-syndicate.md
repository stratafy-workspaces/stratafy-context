## Description

## Overview

Intentionally curate angel investors selected for signaling value rather than capital volume. Research shows comparable companies (Letta, HydraDB) used high-profile angels to create credibility chains that attracted lead institutional investors.

## Scope

- Identify 5-10 target angels known in strategy, operations, and AI spaces
- Prioritise former Chief Strategy Officers, strategy consulting partners, AI infrastructure operators, and well-known founder-angels
- Structure small allocation ($25-50K checks) — value is cap table signal, not capital
- Research credibility chain patterns from comparable raises (Letta, HydraDB, Mem0)
- Develop targeted outreach approach for each angel profile
- Coordinate angel syndicate timing to maximise signal ahead of institutional raise

## Expected Outcomes

- 5-10 signal-value angels committed with small checks
- Cap table that creates credibility and conversation for lead investor outreach
- Industry validation from recognised names in strategy/AI space
- Network effects through angel connections to institutional investors
- Strengthened fundraise narrative with demonstrated insider conviction
