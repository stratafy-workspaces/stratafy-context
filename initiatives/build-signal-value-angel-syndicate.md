# Build Signal-Value Angel Syndicate

## Description
Intentionally curate angel investors for signaling value, not just capital. Research shows Letta stacked Jeff Dean (Google DeepMind), HuggingFace CEO, Runway CEO, dbt Labs CEO. HydraDB got Dropbox co-founder and Gokul Rajaram. These names create credibility chains that attract lead investors. Target: identify 5-10 angels known in strategy/ops/AI space. Prioritise: former Chief Strategy Officers, strategy consulting partners, AI infrastructure operators, and well-known founder-angels. Approach with small allocation ($25-50K checks) — the value is their name on the cap table, not the capital.
