## Overview

Develop a positioning playbook that frames Stratafy as the "invisible infrastructure" layer — always present but never competing with visible workflow tools. Addresses the insight that Stratafy's power is in being the connective tissue, not the front-end.

## Scope

### Messaging Frameworks
- Core invisible infrastructure narrative and elevator pitches
- Audience-specific messaging variants (technical buyers, executives, investors)
- Competitive positioning matrices showing Stratafy's unique layer

### Deliverables
- **Positioning Playbook** — comprehensive messaging guide for all stakeholders
- **Competitive Matrices** — visual frameworks showing where Stratafy sits vs. workflow tools
- **Analyst-Ready Narratives** — polished positioning for industry analyst briefings
- **Internal Alignment Guide** — ensuring consistent messaging across all channels

## Expected Outcomes

- Unified positioning language across sales, marketing, and investor materials
- Clear differentiation from workflow tools and project management platforms
- Analyst-ready narratives for category creation conversations
- Reusable frameworks for partner and coach enablement materials
