## Content
# Corporate Structure & IP Protection

## Objective
Secure unencumbered legal control over Stratafy IP and Cortex shareholding before institutional investor conversations begin.

## Context
Cortex Innovations FZ-LLC (UAE/Sharjah) is currently owned 50/50 between Leonard Cremer and Jacques. The 50/50 structure is intentional — it keeps Cortex below the SARS CFC threshold. Jacques has no claim on Stratafy IP; this is an understood arrangement but is undocumented. The IP assignment and share option agreements must be executed immediately to formalise what is currently only a verbal understanding.

## Sub-Actions

### 1.1 — IP Assignment Agreement with Jacques (IMMEDIATE)
Draft and execute a formal IP assignment agreement confirming all Stratafy IP (software, brand, methodology, MCP architecture) is solely owned by Leonard Cremer. Jacques signs as Cortex co-director/shareholder. Engage a UAE-familiar lawyer to draft. This is independent of all other structural decisions and must happen first.
- **Timeline:** Within 30 days
- **Cost:** Low — standard legal document, a few hours of UAE lawyer time
- **Dependency:** None — do this regardless of all other decisions

### 1.2 — Share Call Option Agreement with Jacques (IMMEDIATE)
Leonard holds a call option to purchase Jacques' 50% in Cortex at nominal value at any future date of Leonard's choosing. Key terms:
- Leonard may nominate any company or trust as the transfer destination at time of exercise — without requiring Jacques' approval, only notification
- Includes right of first refusal (Jacques cannot sell without offering Leonard first)
- Includes drag-along (Jacques must participate in any company transaction on same terms)
- Includes tag-along (Jacques can participate if Leonard sells, keeps it fair)
- This preserves CFC protection while securing Leonard's full operational control
- **Timeline:** Within 30 days, concurrent with IP assignment
- **Same legal engagement** as 1.1

### 1.3 — Evaluate Trust Structure for Share Destination
Explore Rob Horton's Cyprus trust as the vehicle to receive Jacques' shares upon option exercise. Rob is a 5-year EO forum member — trusted relationship, existing international trust structure.
- Review Rob's trust deed for: beneficiary addition provisions, capacity to hold operating company shares, trustee structure
- **Path A:** Rob's Cyprus trust acquires Jacques' 50% (preferred if trust deed allows and Rob is open)
- **Path B:** Identify alternative trust or holding structure as fallback
- Key SARS consideration: if Leonard crosses 50% personal ownership at any point before Delaware migration, CFC rules apply. The trust structure is specifically designed to avoid this.
- **Timeline:** Conversation with Rob within 60 days
- **Decision trigger:** Serious Series A conversation OR Delaware migration — whichever first

### 1.4 — Resolve Uniteam Deregistration Issue
Engage SA accountant or attorney to identify and close the deregistration flag on Uniteam Investments. Ensure Uniteam is in good standing before scaling ZAR invoicing through it.
- **Timeline:** Within 60 days
- **Independent of all other actions**

### 1.5 — Pre-Raise Delaware Migration (Planned — Not Yet Active)

- **Trigger:** First serious institutional investor conversation initiated (Madrona, Sequoia)
- **Timeline:** 60–90 days to complete once triggered
