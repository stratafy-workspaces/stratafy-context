# Pricing Model & Revenue Architecture

## Description
Define and validate Stratafy's pricing model across all customer segments: direct companies, coach-led companies, and coaches. Includes tier structure (Starter/Growth/Scale/Enterprise for companies; Network Member/Professional/Partner for coaches), hybrid pricing philosophy (base subscription + usage-based components), revenue share mechanics (15-20% to coaches on client MRR, 20% to network operator on coach MRR), and unit economics modeling for seed pitch. Key deliverables: validated pricing tiers, revenue share structure with clean layer separation, usage-based component definitions, competitive positioning, and regional pricing decisions.
