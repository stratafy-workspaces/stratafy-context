## Content
## Overview

Define and implement the partner commercial model covering all revenue streams and legal frameworks. This is the business foundation that makes the partner ecosystem commercially viable and legally sound.

## Scope

### Commercial Model Components
- **Setup fees** — One-time onboarding and IP ingestion costs
- **Monthly platform fees** — Ongoing hosting and infrastructure charges
- **Per-seat or per-workspace pricing** — Usage-based scaling model
- **Services referral fees** — Revenue share when plugin interactions convert to in-person engagements

### Legal Framework
- **Partner agreement template** — Standard terms for all partners
- **IP ownership clauses** — Clear delineation of partner IP vs. Stratafy platform IP
- **Custodianship terms** — Stratafy's obligations as IP custodian
- **Data partitioning obligations** — Guarantees on partner data isolation and security

### Validation
- Test pricing with first partner (We Are 52)
- Benchmark against comparable platform partner programs
- Legal review before first agreement signing

## Expected Outcomes

- Standardized partner agreement template ready for signing
- Pricing model validated with first partner
- Clear IP ownership and custodianship framework
- Scalable commercial structure for future partners
