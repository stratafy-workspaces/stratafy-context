## Overview

The sensing and learning system that feeds the outside world and internal performance back into strategic thinking. Signals capture environmental changes, insights synthesise patterns, decisions track responses, and radar scans run AI-powered environmental sweeps.

## Scope

- **Signals**: Capture what's happening — market shifts, competitor moves, internal events, regulatory changes
- **Insights**: AI-assisted synthesis of patterns across multiple signals
- **Decisions**: Track choices made in response to intelligence with rationale and outcomes
- **Radar Scans**: AI-powered environmental sweeps with configurable prompts and sessions
- **Alignment Scans**: Measure coherence across the strategy tree, surfacing contradictions and gaps
- Signal routing, classification, and impact assessment workflows

## Expected Outcomes

- Create continuous environmental awareness that feeds strategic adaptation
- Enable pattern recognition across disparate signals through AI synthesis
- Build institutional memory of decisions and their outcomes for learning loops
- Provide measurable alignment scores that track strategic coherence over time
