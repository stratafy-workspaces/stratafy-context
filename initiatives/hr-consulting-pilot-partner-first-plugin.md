## Overview

Sign and onboard one HR consulting firm as the first partner in the Plugin Ecosystem. Ingest their methodology, build a branded Claude plugin, and deploy it live in at least one client workspace. This validates the full-stack partner model end-to-end.

## Scope

### End-to-End Validation
1. **Partner signing** — Agreement on IP hosting, commercial terms, and pilot scope
2. **IP ingestion** — Capture and structure the partner's HR methodology
3. **Plugin construction** — Build branded Claude-powered plugin from ingested IP
4. **Workspace deployment** — Deploy live in at least one client workspace
5. **Feedback loop** — Document gaps, friction points, and improvement areas

### Technical Requirements
- Secure partitioned storage for partner IP
- Agent construction pipeline converting methodology to live plugin
- Branded partner panel in workspace front-end
- Usage tracking and analytics for partner visibility

### Target
We Are 52 (HR consulting, Cape Town) as anchor partner candidate.

## Expected Outcomes

- Full-stack partner model validated from signing to live deployment
- Case study produced for attracting subsequent partners
- Technical pipeline proven for non-technical firm onboarding
- Commercial model tested with real revenue data
