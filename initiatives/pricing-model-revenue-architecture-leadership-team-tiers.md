## Overview

Three-phase pricing strategy anchored on strategic AI infrastructure positioning. Moves from Pioneer Program (free/low-cost validation) through Infrastructure Tiers to Scale Pricing, framed as hiring AI executives rather than subscribing to software.

## Scope

- **Phase 1 — Pioneer Program (Now > Seed Close)**: 10-15 active workspaces at R0-5K/month for feedback, usage data, and case studies. Coaches get free workspace + 2 client workspaces. STIR at R25K as paid proof point.
- **Phase 2 — Infrastructure Tiers (Post-Seed > Month 12)**: Foundation (R5-10K), Intelligence (R25-50K), Enterprise (R50-100K). Leadership Team framing. Pioneer loyalty discount 20%.
- **Phase 3 — Scale Pricing (Month 12+)**: Usage-based components on tier base, annual contracts, per-seat multiplier for daily execution loop value.
- Deliverables: Pioneer Program doc (done), Phase 2 tier definitions, unit economics model, revenue share agreements, competitive positioning, regional pricing (ZAR primary, USD secondary)

## Expected Outcomes

- Establish pricing that reflects strategic infrastructure value, not SaaS commodity
- Generate early revenue through Pioneer Program to demonstrate commercial traction
- Create clear upgrade paths that align with customer growth and capability depth
- Build unit economics model that supports investor fundraising narrative
