# Pricing Model & Revenue Architecture (Leadership Team Tiers)

## Description
Define and validate Stratafy's pricing model reframed around the Stratafy Leadership Team. The core shift: customers don't buy workspace subscriptions — they hire AI executives.

THREE TIERS:
- Founder Team (R25,000/month): Stratafy CFO + COO + CMO. Full workspace + Finance module + Linear integration. Target: solo founders, pre-seed to Series A.
- Executive Team (R50,000/month): Full C-suite (CFO, COO, CMO, CTO, CPO, CHRO). Cross-executive coordination. Team member access. Target: scaling startups (10-50 people).
- Advisory Executive Suite: Advisory firms deploy Leadership Teams to client workspaces. Branded executive personas with firm's methodology. Revenue share (15-20%) on client workspace subscriptions. Cross-client executive dashboard.

PRICING PHILOSOPHY: The workspace is free infrastructure. The executives are the product you pay for. This reframes from "software subscription" to "fractional team hire" — a mental model customers already understand.

UNIT ECONOMICS: R25k/month for three AI executives is less than a part-time bookkeeper in Cape Town. R50k/month for six executives is less than one junior hire. The value comparison sells itself.

REVENUE SHARE MECHANICS: Advisory firms earn 15-20% on client MRR they bring to platform. Coach networks earn 20% on coach MRR. Clean layer separation maintained.

KEY DELIVERABLES: Validated tier structure, executive-team-based pricing page, revenue share agreements, competitive positioning vs. fractional exec marketplaces, regional pricing (ZAR primary, USD for international).
