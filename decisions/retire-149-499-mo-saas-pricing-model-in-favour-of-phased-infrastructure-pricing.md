# Retire $149–499/mo SaaS pricing model in favour of phased infrastructure pricing

## Description
The February 2026 Stratafy Pricing Model document defined four tiers at $0 / $149 / $249 / $499 per month — positioning Stratafy as a mid-market SaaS application competing with Cascade, Quantive, and Weekdone. Subsequent market research (AI Infrastructure Funding Landscape doc, HydraDB competitive analysis) and the depth of the Strategic AI Infrastructure strategy reveal this pricing is approximately 10x too low. The market pays $249–$5,000/month for generic context infrastructure; Stratafy delivers domain-specific strategic intelligence infrastructure on top of that.\n\nThe decision: retire the $149–499/mo model entirely and replace it with a three-phase pricing strategy:\n- Phase 1 (Pioneer Program): R0–5K/month to acquire 10–15 active workspaces for feedback and usage data\n- Phase 2 (Infrastructure Tiers): Foundation R5–10K/month, Intelligence R25–50K/month, Enterprise R50–100K/month\n- Phase 3 (Scale Pricing): Usage-based components, annual contracts with expansion, partner channel economics

## Research
AI Infrastructure Funding Landscape doc: median AI seed $4.6M, HydraDB $6.5M pre-seed, context infra category at $45M+ aggregate funding. HydraDB pricing: $249/$999/$5K per month for generic context. Zep: $1M revenue with 5 people. STIR paying R25K/month validates the price floor. Insight: "HydraDB pricing sets a floor — generic context infra at $249–$5K/month validates Stratafy's pricing power."

## Decision
Retire the $149–499/mo SaaS pricing model. Adopt a three-phase pricing strategy: Pioneer Program (Phase 1) for early adoption, Infrastructure Tiers (Phase 2) post-seed, and Usage-Based Scale Pricing (Phase 3) at maturity. The February pricing doc will be rewritten to reflect this model.

## Rationale
1. The Strategic AI Infrastructure strategy has built capabilities (provenance, temporal evolution, semantic retrieval, alignment scanning) that position Stratafy as infrastructure, not SaaS application software. Infrastructure commands infrastructure pricing.\n\n2. Market benchmarks confirm this: HydraDB charges $249–$5K/month for generic context storage. Mem0 raised $24M, Letta raised $10M at $70M valuation — all horizontal context infra plays. Stratafy's domain-specific version should command at least comparable pricing.\n\n3. STIR Collective already pays R25,000/month, validating the higher price point in practice.\n\n4. The immediate priority is workspace adoption for fundraise readiness, not revenue maximisation. Phase 1 deliberately subsidizes access to generate the usage data and case studies needed for the $5–7M seed raise.\n\n5. The $149/mo price anchors prospects at a level that undervalues the infrastructure by ~10x and would train the market to expect SaaS-level pricing for infrastructure-level value.
