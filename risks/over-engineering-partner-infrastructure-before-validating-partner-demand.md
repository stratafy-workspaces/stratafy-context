# Over-engineering partner infrastructure before validating partner demand

## Description
Building the partner IP ingestion pipeline, agent construction tooling, and marketplace before finding a willing first partner could result in significant engineering investment with no partner to use it. The build-then-recruit sequence is higher risk than recruiting a committed pilot partner first and building to their specific needs.

## Assessment
- **Likelihood**: medium
- **Impact**: high
- **Risk Score**: 6
- **Type**: execution
- **Source**: internal
- **Status**: active

## Mitigation
- **Status**: identified
- **Plan**: Sequence the work: recruit and LOI the HR consulting pilot partner BEFORE building the ingestion pipeline. Use the Finance Director plugin as proof of concept in partner conversations. Only invest in generalised infrastructure once the first partner is committed.
- **Review Date**: Not scheduled
