# Partner IP dispute — allegation of misuse or exposure of hosted methodology

## Description
A partner firm could share their IP with Stratafy for hosting and agent construction, then later allege that Stratafy has used their methodology in other contexts, trained models on it, or exposed it to other partners. Even if technically unfounded, this allegation could be reputationally damaging and legally costly, particularly as Stratafy seeks institutional investment.

## Assessment
- **Likelihood**: low
- **Impact**: critical
- **Risk Score**: 4
- **Type**: regulatory
- **Source**: external
- **Status**: active

## Mitigation
- **Status**: identified
- **Plan**: Implement contractual custodianship agreements with explicit IP ownership clauses. Build technical partition architecture (separate vector stores per partner, no cross-partner data access). Consider independent audit capability. Engage legal counsel before onboarding first partner.
- **Review Date**: Not scheduled
