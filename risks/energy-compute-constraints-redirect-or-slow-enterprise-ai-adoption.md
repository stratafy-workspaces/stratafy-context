# Energy/compute constraints redirect or slow enterprise AI adoption

## Description
IEA projects data centers doubling energy consumption by 2026. 40% expected to use onsite power generation by 2030. US chip export restrictions fragmenting global compute access. Goldman Sachs estimates $200B in AI infrastructure investment in 2025. Two scenarios: (1) Constraints slow enterprise AI adoption broadly — reducing demand for governance tooling, softening Stratafy's urgency narrative. (2) Constraints force disciplined AI deployment — companies must strategically prioritize which agents get compute, making Stratafy's strategic allocation role more critical. Scenario 2 is more likely based on current enterprise commitment levels (100% expanding agentic AI per CrewAI survey), but scenario 1 could materialize if energy costs spike unexpectedly or regulatory intervention constrains AI compute access.
