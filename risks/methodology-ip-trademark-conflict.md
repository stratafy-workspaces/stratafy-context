# Methodology IP/Trademark Conflict

## Description
Scaling Up, EOS Worldwide, or other methodology organizations could view Stratafy's use of their methodology frameworks and certification references as unauthorized or competitive. A cease-and-desist during Phase 2 expansion would be catastrophic — forcing a rebrand of methodology-specific features and potentially alienating coaches who joined for methodology-specific value.

## Assessment
- **Likelihood**: medium
- **Impact**: critical
- **Risk Score**: 8
- **Type**: regulatory
- **Source**: external
- **Status**: active

## Mitigation
- **Status**: identified
- **Plan**: Position as complementary from day one. Seek informal blessing before formal partnership. Avoid implying official endorsement. Have methodology-agnostic fallback framing ready. Initiate conversations with methodology leaders during Phase 1 before scaling.
- **Review Date**: Not scheduled
