# Directory Model Replicated Without Intelligence Moat

## Description
Methodology HQs or well-funded competitors see the coach directory model working and build their own version. Scaling Up could launch an official coach marketplace. EOS Worldwide could integrate tech into their existing ecosystem. Without the strategic intelligence layer shipping alongside the directory, Stratafy's offering is replicable — it's just a better listing site.

## Assessment
- **Likelihood**: medium
- **Impact**: high
- **Risk Score**: 6
- **Type**: competitive
- **Source**: external
- **Status**: active

## Mitigation
- **Status**: identified
- **Plan**: Ensure Coach Portfolio Intelligence Platform ships in parallel with Phase 2. The directory is the acquisition layer; intelligence is the retention layer. Together they create a moat of accumulated strategic data across hundreds of workspaces that takes years to replicate. Track this dependency explicitly.
- **Review Date**: Not scheduled
