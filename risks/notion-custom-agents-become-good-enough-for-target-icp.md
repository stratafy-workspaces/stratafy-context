# Notion Custom Agents become "good enough" for target ICP

## Description
Notion launched Custom Agents on February 24 2026 with native MCP integrations, scheduled triggers, multi-step workflows, and team-wide deployment on Business plans at $20/user/month. A sophisticated operator like Sam (Missing Link) could build a strategy execution system within Notion that approximates much of Stratafy's daily workflow layer — morning briefings, ICP guardrails, weekly reviews, team context sharing. If the perceived value gap between Stratafy and a well-built Notion agent system is not demonstrably clear within the first 1-2 sessions, prospects will default to extending their existing Notion investment rather than adopting a new platform. The risk compounds as Notion continues shipping: multi-model AI (GPT-5, Claude Opus 4.1, o3), MCP connections to 30+ external tools, and collaborative agent infrastructure. Notion has 100M+ users, an established trust relationship, and a workflow that teams already live in. The 'good enough' scenario does not require Notion to match Stratafy — it only requires that the marginal value of Stratafy is not visible enough to justify the switching cost.

## Mitigation
- **Status**: in_progress
- **Plan**: 1. Reframe the competition: Stratafy as the strategic ground truth layer that makes Notion agents worth having — not a replacement. 2. Make the five structural gaps undeniable in every demo: alignment scanning, typed strategic entities, cross-portfolio intelligence, provenance, maintenance durability. 3. Build and protect the alignment scan as the killer feature Notion cannot replicate. 4. Demonstrate output quality difference side-by-side using prospect's own content in session 2. 5. Target the May 4 2026 Notion credit pricing event as a conversion window. 6. Build and present a TCO model in every engagement: manager time (hrs/month × hourly cost) + Notion credits + tool fragmentation overhead vs Stratafy subscription. The CRM analogy is powerful — Sam replaced ParkDrive with a custom Notion CRM. Ask her: how much time does that take to maintain? What happens when it breaks? Notion is now doing the same thing to her strategy infrastructure. 7. Frame complexity growth as a cost accelerator: every new agent, database, team member, and strategy update compounds the maintenance burden. Stratafy's cost is fixed; Notion's hidden cost grows with the business.
