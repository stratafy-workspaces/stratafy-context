# Lead-to-Engagement Conversion Gap

## Description
Directory generates inbound leads but coaches fail to convert them into paying engagements. Without conversion enablement (first call playbooks, demo scripts, conversion tracking), coaches blame the platform for bad leads rather than recognizing a skills/process gap. This creates negative word-of-mouth in methodology networks and coach churn.

## Assessment
- **Likelihood**: high
- **Impact**: high
- **Risk Score**: 9
- **Type**: execution
- **Source**: internal
- **Status**: active

## Mitigation
- **Status**: identified
- **Plan**: Build coach sales enablement kit as Phase 1 deliverable (not Phase 3). Include first call playbook, methodology-specific demo scripts, and conversion tracking dashboard. Run monthly peer learning calls. Track lead-to-engagement conversion by coach to identify who needs support.
- **Review Date**: Not scheduled
