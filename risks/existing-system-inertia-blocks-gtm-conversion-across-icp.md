# Existing System Inertia blocks GTM conversion across ICP

## Description
The majority of companies in the target ICP (10-200 employees, AI-forward) will have already built bespoke strategy systems in Notion, Confluence, SharePoint, or Google Drive + Claude by the time they encounter Stratafy. They have invested significant time, emotional capital, and operational dependency in these systems. Without a clear reframe and a frictionless ingestion story, sales cycles will stall at this objection in the majority of conversations. Risk materialisation looks like: prospects acknowledging the value but deprioritising migration, choosing to extend their existing system instead, or stalling at the proof-of-concept stage indefinitely. This is not a low-probability risk — it is near-certain to occur and will be the primary GTM friction point across all channels.

## Assessment
- **Likelihood**: high
- **Impact**: high
- **Risk Score**: 9
- **Type**: market
- **Source**: external
- **Status**: active

## Mitigation
- **Status**: in_progress
- **Plan**: 1. Reframe positioning from 'replacement' to 'promotion' — Stratafy promotes existing content into agent-readable infrastructure, it doesn't replace it. 2. Make ingestion fast and frictionless — the transition from Notion/Google Drive to Stratafy must feel like an upgrade, not a migration. 3. Build a 'before and after' case study using Missing Link as the proof point. 4. Develop a one-paragraph answer to the 'what does Stratafy do?' question that preempts this objection before the demo. 5. Lead every sales conversation with the problem (document model breaks under agent load and team scale), not the product.
- **Review Date**: Not scheduled
