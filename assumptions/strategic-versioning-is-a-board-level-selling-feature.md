# Strategic versioning is a board-level selling feature

## Description
Board members and investors will pay a premium for "show me how our strategy evolved this year" — the ability to see strategy diffs, track when pivots happened, and understand the reasoning behind strategic evolution. This is the kind of feature that moves Stratafy from "useful tool" to "board-level infrastructure" and justifies enterprise pricing ($5K+/mo). If wrong, time-travel is a nice-to-have rather than a conversion driver, and engineering effort should go elsewhere.

- **Confidence**: hypothesis

- **Method**: Present the concept in upcoming board-level demos and investor conversations. Track whether "strategy evolution over time" generates excitement or indifference. Ask: "How do you currently track how your strategy has changed?" — if they have no answer, the need is real.
