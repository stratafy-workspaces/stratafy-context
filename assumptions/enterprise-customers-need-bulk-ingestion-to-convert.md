# Enterprise customers need bulk ingestion to convert

## Description
Without a fast onboarding path that ingests existing strategic artifacts (board decks, OKR spreadsheets, annual plans), enterprise adoption friction is too high. Companies with existing strategic architecture won't manually recreate it in Stratafy. The "upload your board deck, get a strategy tree in 10 minutes" experience is the enterprise conversion accelerator. If this assumption is wrong, manual setup with coaching may be sufficient — but it limits scalability and increases CAC.

## Assessment
- **Confidence**: likely
- **Type**: market
- **Impact if Wrong**: high
- **Status**: active

## Validation
- **Method**: Test with next 3 enterprise prospects: offer manual setup vs. assisted ingestion. Measure time-to-value and conversion rate. Ask directly: "Would you sign up faster if we could import your existing strategy docs?"
