# 18-24 month category-creation window is real

## Description
The strategic operating system category is forming now and first movers will define it before incumbents (Microsoft, Salesforce, etc.) mobilize. This assumes: (1) incumbents are 18-24 months behind on AI-native strategy infrastructure, (2) the market will consolidate around a category leader rather than fragmenting, (3) developer/enterprise adoption patterns favor early definers. If wrong, we're either too early (market isn't ready) or too late (incumbents already building).

## Assessment
- **Confidence**: validated
- **Type**: market
- **Impact if Wrong**: critical
- **Status**: active

## Validation
- **Method**: Track incumbent announcements (Microsoft, Salesforce, ServiceNow) for strategic AI governance features. Monitor VC investment in adjacent categories. Track enterprise demand signals for AI governance/context solutions.
- **Validated At**: Not yet validated
- **Evidence**: PREVIOUS: AI agent adoption accelerating, no incumbent positioned strategic OS product yet.

UPDATED Feb 16, 2026 (Radar Scan):

CONFIRMING EVIDENCE:
- CrewAI 2026 survey: 100% of enterprises expanding agentic AI, 65% already using agents, 81% scaling across teams
- Gartner: 40% of enterprise workflows will involve autonomous agents by 2026
- 74% view production deployment as critical (not pilot, not experimental — production)
- ServiceNow-Kearney, Snowflake-OpenAI, SAP all building operational agent platforms — confirming market timing
- None occupy the strategic context layer — confirming positioning gap

COMPETITIVE WINDOW EVIDENCE:
- Operational platforms (ServiceNow, Snowflake, SAP) are 12-18 months from maturity
- Consulting firms (Deloitte, McKinsey) partnering on AI governance but at advisory level, not infrastructure
- AMD-TCS bringing AI infra to emerging markets — expanding total addressable market

RISK FACTOR:
- Energy/compute constraints could slow adoption (IEA: data centers doubling energy by 2026, chip export restrictions)
- Net assessment: constraints more likely to force disciplined deployment (favoring governance) than kill adoption entirely

CONCLUSION: Category window is validated and narrowing. The 18-24 months started ~6 months ago. Remaining window: 12-18 months from today (Feb 2026).
