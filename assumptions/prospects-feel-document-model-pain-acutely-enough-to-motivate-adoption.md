# Prospects feel document model pain acutely enough to motivate adoption

## Description
The core sales argument assumes that prospects who have built Notion-based strategy systems will *feel* the pain of context fragmentation, strategy decay, agent incompatibility, and maintenance concentration acutely enough that it motivates them to switch. The risk is that companies may not connect these symptoms to the infrastructure cause — they may blame the AI, the team, or the strategy itself, and rebuild the Notion workspace rather than adopt Stratafy. Pain must be legible, not just logical.

## Assessment
- **Confidence**: hypothesis
- **Type**: market
- **Impact if Wrong**: critical
- **Status**: active

## Validation
- **Method**: Test in sales conversations: ask prospects directly whether they experience context loss across team AI sessions, strategy drift between reviews, and inconsistent agent outputs. Track whether prospects identify the infrastructure cause vs. attributing symptoms elsewhere. Missing Link engagement is the first live test.
- **Validated At**: Not yet validated
- **Evidence**: No evidence recorded
