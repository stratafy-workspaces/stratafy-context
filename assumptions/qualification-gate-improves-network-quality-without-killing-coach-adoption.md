# Qualification gate improves network quality without killing coach adoption

## Description
Requiring coaches to operate Stratafy for their own practice for ~5 weeks before directory listing will improve quality (authentic expertise, better conversion rates, higher directory trust) without creating unacceptable friction that causes coach drop-off during onboarding. The gate must feel like investment in their success, not a bureaucratic hurdle.

## Assessment
- **Confidence**: hypothesis
- **Type**: operational
- **Impact if Wrong**: high
- **Status**: active

## Validation
- **Method**: Track Phase 1 founding coach cohort: what percentage complete the 5-week gate? At what stage do drop-offs occur? Do gate-completers convert leads at higher rates than a hypothetical ungated baseline? Adjust gate requirements if completion rate drops below 70%.
