# Consulting firms will trust Stratafy to host their proprietary methodology

## Description
The partner model only works if firms are willing to hand over their methodology documents and frameworks for hosting inside Stratafy's infrastructure. Many boutique consultancies treat this IP as their most sensitive competitive asset. They need to be convinced that Stratafy is a custodian, not an owner — and that their IP is technically isolated from other partners and from Stratafy itself.

- **Confidence**: hypothesis
- **Type**: market
- **Impact if Wrong**: critical
- **Status**: active

- **Method**: Conduct structured conversations with 3-5 HR consulting principals. Test willingness with a proposed custodianship agreement and technical isolation explainer. Measure response to the IP hosting framing vs self-hosted alternative.
