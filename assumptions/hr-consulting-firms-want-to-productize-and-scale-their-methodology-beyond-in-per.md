# HR consulting firms want to productize and scale their methodology beyond in-person delivery

## Description
The value proposition for consulting firms is that a Stratafy plugin lets them productize their methodology, scale delivery beyond in-person time, and generate qualified leads for their services. This assumes firms have genuine desire to scale — some boutique consultancies deliberately stay small and may not see plugin distribution as desirable. The HR consulting category needs to be scoped to growth-oriented firms, not lifestyle practices.
