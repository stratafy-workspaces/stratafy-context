# Three-tool stack is acceptable adoption friction

## Description
Customers will accept a three-tool stack (Claude + Stratafy + Linear) without significant adoption friction or cost objection. This assumes: (1) Linear's free tier is sufficient for most early-stage target customers, (2) the value of each layer is clear enough to justify separate tools, (3) advisory firm channel partners won't balk at deploying three tools to their clients, and (4) the AI Operations Stack framing (each layer has a distinct role) makes the multi-tool approach feel intentional rather than fragmented.

## Assessment
- **Confidence**: hypothesis
- **Type**: market
- **Impact if Wrong**: high
- **Status**: active

- **Method**: Track during STIR onboarding (Feb 17+) and ScaleUp demo (Feb 23).
