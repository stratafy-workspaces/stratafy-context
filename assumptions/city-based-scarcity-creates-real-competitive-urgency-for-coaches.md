# City-based scarcity creates real competitive urgency for coaches

## Description
City-based exclusivity and limited founding spots will create genuine urgency among coaches. The FOMO mechanics depend on coaches viewing their city as competitive territory worth claiming. If coaches don't perceive territorial competition, the scarcity model collapses into just another sign-up flow.

## Assessment
- **Confidence**: hypothesis
- **Type**: market
- **Impact if Wrong**: high
- **Status**: active

## Validation
- **Method**: Track outreach response rates and time-to-decision for coaches in cities with vs without competing interest. If scarcity messaging doesn't accelerate sign-ups, test alternative urgency levers.
